DTC P0685 (Flash Code 416) ECM Power Relay Control Circuit/Open (4JJ1)

1. DTC P0685 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JJ1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Inspection of ECM main relay

Turn OFF the ignition switch for 30 seconds.

Replace the ECM main relay with a heater relay or a known good relay.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Check for a DTC using a scan tool.

If a DTC is set, go to Inspection of ECM main relay power supply and ground.

If a DTC is not set, replace the ECM main relay. Go to Repair verification.

3. Inspection of ECM main relay power supply and ground

Inspect the connection status of the ECM main relay ground terminal, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect the ECM 40 A slow blow fuse, and replace as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the ECM 40 A slow blow fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If it is normal, go to Inspection of ECM main relay circuit.

4. Inspection of ECM main relay circuit

Turn OFF the ignition switch

Disconnect the ECM harness connector.

Inspect the connection status of the ECM main relay control circuit and the battery power supply circuit at the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect each relay circuit for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

ECM main relay control circuit.
Battery power supply circuit between the ECM main relay and the ECM.
Battery power supply circuit between the ECM 40 A slow blow fuse and the ECM main relay.
ECM main relay ground circuit.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

5. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P0685 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn the ignition switch OFF for 30 seconds or longer.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Check for a DTC using a scan tool.

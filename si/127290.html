Front door assembly disassembly (All models)

1. Power window switch removal

1. Disconnect the harness connector from the power window switch.

2. Door mirror cover removal

1. Remove the door mirror cover from the front door.

Note

  • Pull out the clip, and remove the cover in the upward direction.

  1. Door mirror cover

3. Door pull case removal

1. Remove the door pull case from the front door.

Note

  • Open the cover with a small-sized flat-blade screwdriver, etc., so as not to damage the door pull case, and remove the screw.

  1. Door pull case
  2. Screw
  3. Cover

4. Front door trim panel removal

1. Remove the front door trim panel from the front door.

Note

  • Open the cover with a small-sized flat-blade screwdriver, etc., so as not to damage the front door trim panel, and remove the screw.
  • Pull out the clips at 9 locations on the front door trim panel.

  1. Clip position
  2. Cover
  3. Screw

5. Door mirror assembly removal

1. Remove the door mirror assembly from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the 3 fixing bolts, and disconnect the connector.

  1. Bolt
  2. Connector

6. Outer waist seal removal

1. Remove the outer waist seal from the front door.

7. Bracket removal

1. Remove the bracket from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 screws.

8. Door speaker removal

1. Remove the door speaker from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the fastening screw and disconnect the connector.

9. Weather strip removal

1. Remove the weather strip from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the clip.

10. Waterproof sheet removal

1. Remove the waterproof sheet from the front door.

Note

  • When removing the sheet, be careful not to damage it.

11. Inside handle removal

1. Disconnect the inside handle from the door lock cable.

12. Front door glass removal

1. Remove the front door glass from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 bolts that are put through the access hole, and pull out the front door glass upward.

13. Window regulator removal

1. Remove the window regulator from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the 4 fixing bolts and 2 fixing nuts.
  • Disconnect the power window motor connector.

14. Glass run removal

1. Remove the glass-run from the front door.

Note

  • Pull it out from the door frame of the channel.

15. Glass-run rear channel removal

1. Remove the glass-run rear channel from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the bolt.

16. Door lock assembly removal

1. Remove the door lock assembly from the front door.

Note

  • Remove the 3 fixing screws and the bolt.
  • Disconnect the linkage between the outside handle and the key cylinder.
  • Disconnect the harness connector.

17. Key cylinder removal

1. Remove the key cylinder from the outside handle.

Note

  • Remove the cover and remove the fixing screw.

  1. Cover
  2. Screw
  3. Key cylinder

18. Outside handle removal

1. Remove the outside handle from the front door.

19. Door harness removal

1. Remove the door harness from the front door.

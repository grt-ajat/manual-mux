Lost communication with the PESS control unit (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. Lost communication with the PESS control unit description of function

The ECM, TCM, DRM, TCCM, EHCU, PESS control unit, SRS control unit, BCM, and instrument panel cluster communicate with the scan tool via the CAN communication bus. The EGR valve and auto A/C control unit use the same CAN communication bus when the vehicle is running, but do not use it for communicating with the scan tool.

2. Lost communication with the PESS control unit

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "229.Description Security and Locks Control 42C.Functional Inspection(Passive Entry and Start System) diagnostic system check-passive entry and start system".

2. Inspection of PESS control unit harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the PESS control unit harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Inspect the PESS (+B) 10 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the PESS (+B) 10 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the PESS control unit harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the battery power supply circuit and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair the open circuit or high resistance in the battery power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the battery power supply circuit and the ground circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to CAN serial data circuit inspection.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of PESS control unit ground circuit.

5. Inspection of PESS control unit ground circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the PESS control unit switch ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, repair any corrosion or tightness of the PESS control unit ground terminal. Go to Repair verification.

6. Inspection of CAN serial data circuit

Inspect the connection status of the CAN circuit junction connector between the PESS control unit and the DLC, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the CAN circuit between the PESS control unit and the DLC or for a short circuit between CAN circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

7. PESS control unit replacement

Replace the PESS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit setting".

8. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected fuses and harness connectors.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Perform one of the following operations.

Press the engine start/stop button.
Grip the driver side outside handle.
Press the driver side outside handle switch.
Press the tail gate opener switch.
Press the tail gate lock button.
Open the door and close the door.

Verify that communication can be established with the PESS control units using a scan tool.

If communication cannot be established, go to Inspection of PESS control unit harness connector.

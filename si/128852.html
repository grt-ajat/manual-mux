Power steering oil pump (with variable capacity pump) installation (All models)

1. Power steering oil pump assembly installation

1. Install the power steering oil pump to the oil pump bracket.

Tightening torque： 25 N・m { 2.5 kgf・m / 18 lb・ft }

2. Connect the flexible hose to the power steering oil pump.

Tightening torque： 8 N・m { 0.8 kgf・m / 71 lb・in }

2. Suction hose connect

1. Connect the suction hose to the power steering oil pump.

3. Power steering fluid air bleed

1. Drain air from the power steering fluid.

Note

  • When removing the air from the system, power steering fluid can be added to the power steering system at any time, but only a type of power steering fluid specified in the general information should be used.
  • Fill the pump fluid reservoir with the power steering fluid up to the appropriate level, and let the power steering fluid settle for at least 2 minutes.
  • Start the engine and keep it running for at least 2 seconds.
  • Then, stop the engine without turning the steering wheel.

2. Replenish the reservoir tank with the power steering fluid.

Note

  • If necessary, add more power steering fluid.
  • Repeat the procedure above until the engine starts and the fluid level is stabilized.
  • Lift up and support the front of the vehicle to keep the wheel away from the ground.

3. Start the engine.

Note

  • Slowly turn the steering wheel to the left and the right, and let it lightly contact with the wheel stop.
  • If necessary, add the power steering fluid.
  • After turning the steering wheel to the full steering state 2 - 3 times, lower the vehicle, and set the steering wheel to the position for advancing straight forward.

4. Stop the engine.

Note

  • Check the fluid level, and add it as necessary.
  • When the power steering fluid is extremely bubbled, stabilize the vehicle for a few minutes.
  • Then, repeat the procedure above.

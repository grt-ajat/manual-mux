DTC B1102 ACC Power Source Relay Response (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. DTC B1102 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "229.Description Security and Locks Control 42C.Functional Inspection(Passive Entry and Start System) diagnostic system check-passive entry and start system".

2. Prioritized DTC

If DTC B1101 is set, go to the DTC B1101 diagnosis.

3. ACC relay inspection

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Replace the ACC relay with a known good relay.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is set, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

If the DTC is not set, replace the ACC relay. Go to Repair verification.

4. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the ACC relay.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Accessory Switch display on the scan tool.

If OFF is displayed, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If ON is displayed, go to Inspection for short circuit in ACC feedback signal circuit.

5. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a fused jumper wire to the ACC relay harness connector switch side circuit.

Observe the Accessory Switch display on the scan tool.

If OFF is displayed, go to Inspection for open circuit in ACC relay circuit.

If ON is displayed, repair the open circuit in the ACC relay ground circuit or poor connection with the ground terminal. Go to Repair verification.

6. Inspection for short circuit in ACC feedback signal circuit

Inspect the ACC feedback signal circuit between the PESS control unit and the ACC relay for a short to the power supply circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

7. Inspection for open circuit in ACC relay circuit

Inspect the slow blow fuse, and replace as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuits relating to the slow blow fuse. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect each circuit for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

ACC relay power supply circuit between the slow blow fuse and ACC relay
ACC feedback signal circuit between the PESS control unit and ACC relay

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit harness connector inspection.

8. PESS control unit harness connector inspection

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the PESS control unit harness connector.

Inspect the connection status of the PESS control unit harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

9. PESS control unit replacement

Replace the PESS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit setting".

2. DTC B1102 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch and wait for 10 seconds.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

Automatic transmission control cable removal (TB-50LS)

1. Battery ground cable disconnect

1. Place the chock blocks at front and rear of tire.

2. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Note

  • Move the select lever to the N position.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

2. Console box removal

1. Remove the shift console cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to the center console type
  • Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector.

2. Remove the 4WD switch from the console box.

Note

  • Disconnect the 4WD switch connector.

3. Remove the console box from the floor.

Note

  • Remove the 2 clips and the 1 screw.

  1. Shift console cover
  2. Cover (2WD models)
  3. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • Open the console box cover and remove 2 screws.
  • Remove the console box backward.

  1. 4WD switch
  2. Screw
  3. Parking brake cover
  4. Console box
  5. Clip

3. Automatic transmission control cable removal

1. Disconnect the automatic transmission control cable from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

Note

  • Press the cap part of the automatic transmission control cable.

2. Disconnect the automatic transmission control cable from the select control lever.

3. Disconnect the automatic transmission control cable from the bracket.

  1. Bracket, lower
  2. Automatic transmission control cable

4. Remove the clip from the bracket.

Note

  • Remove the clip of LH side and disconnect the automatic transmission control cable.

5. Remove the clip from the bracket.

Note

  • Remove the clip of upper side and disconnect the automatic transmission control cable.
  • Pull the automatic transmission control cable free from the bottom of the vehicle.

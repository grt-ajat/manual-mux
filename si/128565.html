DTC B2555 Map Light / Dome Light Circuit Malfunction (BCM)

1. DTC B2555 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "223.Description Body Control 36C.Functional Inspection(BCM with PESS) diagnostic system check-body controls".

Refer to "223.Description Body Control 36C.Functional Inspection(BCM with Keyless Entry) diagnostic system check-body controls".

2. Inspection of dome light

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the dome light.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, replace the dome light. Go to Repair verification.

If a DTC is set, go to Inspection of map light.

3. Inspection of map light

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the map light.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, replace the map light. Go to Repair verification.

If a DTC is set, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

4. Circuit diagnosis 1

Connect a test lamp between the interior light control high side circuit of the dome light and map light harness connector and the power supply circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for short circuit in high side circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

5. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the interior light control low side circuit of the dome light and map light harness connector and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for short circuit in low side circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of interior light control circuit.

6. Inspection for short circuit in high side circuit

Inspect for a short to ground in the interior light control high side circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to BCM replacement.

7. Inspection for short circuit in low side circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the interior light control low side circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to BCM replacement.

8. Inspection of interior light control circuit

Inspect for a short circuit between interior light control high and low side circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to BCM replacement.

9. BCM replacement

Replace the BCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM setting".

2. DTC B2555 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Open and close the driver side door

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

DTC B0012 Default Transponder Detected (Immobilizer)

1. DTC B0012 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic System Check

Refer to "229.Description Security and Locks Control 42C.Functional Inspection(Immobilizer) diagnostic system check-immobilizer controls".

2. ICU setting

Write the information for the transponder key into the ICU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to the following items of "ICU setting".

Erase Transponder-keys
Program Transponder-Keys

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) ICU （Immobilizer with Keyless Entry） setting".

2. DTC B0012 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

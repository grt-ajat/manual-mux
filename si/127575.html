SRS airbag warning light always on (All models)

1. SRS airbag warning light always on description of function

When the ignition switch is turned to ON, ignition voltage is supplied to the SRS control unit through the SRS fuse. At this time, the SRS control unit causes the SRS airbag warning light to flash 7 times to perform a bulb check. Afterwards, the SRS control unit turns the SRS airbag warning light off if a SRC is not detected. If the SRS control unit power system has an open circuit or a poor connection intermittently occurs, a DTC will not be set and the SRS airbag warning light will flash 10 times.

Caution

  • To prevent the replacement SRS system parts from malfunctioning and generating a battery short circuit, remove the SRS fuse from the fuse relay box, or remove the battery ground cable from the battery before replacing the parts.

Warning

  • During the maintenance procedure, be careful not to hit or shake the SRS control unit when handling it.
  • If the SRS control unit is not properly installed to the vehicle, the SRS control unit could be activated when the ignition switch is turned ON, which could cause deployment of the SRS airbag and result in personal injury.

2. SRS airbag warning light always on

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "209.Description Restraints 22C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-SRS controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 15 seconds.

Disconnect the SRS control unit harness connector.

Connect a DMM between the SRS airbag warning light circuit and the ground circuit.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the voltage display on the DMM.

Standard： 3.5 V

If the reading is less than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in warning light circuit.

If the reading is more than the specified value, go to Inspection of SRS control unit harness connector.

3. Inspection for open circuit in warning light circuit

Inspect the SRS airbag warning light circuit for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector.

4. Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the instrument panel cluster harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector.

5. Instrument panel cluster replacement

Replace the instrument panel cluster. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster setting".

6. Inspection of SRS control unit harness connector

Inspect the SRS control unit harness connector for poor connections.

Inspect the SRS control unit harness connector terminal for corrosion.

If a malfunction is found in the harness connector, repair or replace it. Go to Repair verification.

If the harness connector is normal, go to SRS control unit replacement.

7. SRS control unit replacement

Replace the SRS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit installation".

8. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected fuses and harness connectors.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 15 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the SRS airbag warning light stays illuminated, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

If the SRS airbag warning light flashes 7 times when the ignition switch is ON, go to Diagnostic system check-SRS controls.

Check transmission warning light inoperative (TB-50LS)

1. Check transmission warning light inoperative description of function

The check trans warning light comes on when the ignition switch is turned ON, and then it goes off if any DTC has not been detected. When the TCM detects the "ON" signal of the ignition switch, it lights the warning light for approximately 3 seconds for a valve check.

Function of check trans warning light
The warning light is installed inside the instrument panel cluster and flashes when a malfunction occurs to notify the driver that an inspection and repair must be performed as soon as possible.
If the check trans warning light remains flashing responding to an instruction from the TCM, a DTC has been detected.

Lighting conditions of check trans warning light
The warning light illuminates for 3 seconds after the ignition switch is turned ON.
When the TCM detects a malfunction, the warning light remains flashing.

2. Check transmission warning light inoperative

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Inspection for short circuit in check trans warning light circuit

Inspect the METER 10 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the METER 10 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the METER 10 A fuse.

Disconnect the TCM harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the check trans warning light control circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 1 V

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, repair a short to the power supply circuit in the check trans warning light circuit. Go to Repair verification.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Install the METER 10 A fuse.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a fused jumper wire between the check trans warning light control circuit and the body ground.

Confirm illumination of the check trans warning light.

If the check trans warning light does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the check trans warning light illuminates, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the harness connector from the instrument panel cluster.

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit of the instrument panel cluster harness connector and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair an open circuit or high resistance in the instrument panel cluster power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for open circuit in check trans warning light circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in check trans warning light short circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the check trans warning light circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector.

6. Inspection of TCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to TCM Replacement.

7. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

8. Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the instrument panel cluster harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the instrument panel cluster. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster setting".

9. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the fuses and harness connectors that have been disconnected.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Verify that the check trans warning light illuminates for 3 seconds.

If it does not illuminate, go to Inspection for short circuit in check trans warning light circuit.

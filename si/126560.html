DTC P0045 (Flash Code 33) Turbocharger Boost Control Solenoid Circuit (4JJ1)

1. DTC P0045 description of DTC

The opening degree of the turbocharger variable nozzle is controlled by the ECM. The ECM operates the turbocharger control solenoid valve based on boost pressure and controls the variable nozzle by way of a vacuum. When load is not applied to the engine, the variable nozzle is open and there is no boost pressure. When load is applied to the engine, the ECM controls the control solenoid valve and closes the variable nozzle. As a result, boost pressure is increased. The ECM controls changes in boost pressure according to the load requirements of the engine.
The ECM uses pulse width modulation to control the opening and closing of the solenoid valve.
If the ECM detects an open circuit or a short circuit in the solenoid valve circuit, the DTC is set.

2. DTC P0045 condition for setting the DTC

The ignition switch is ON.
Either of the following conditions is met:
The ECM detects a low voltage status of the turbocharger control solenoid circuit when the solenoid is commanded OFF.
The ECM detects a high voltage status of the turbocharger control solenoid circuit when the solenoid is commanded ON.

3. DTC P0045 action taken when the DTC sets

The ECM illuminates the MIL when the diagnostic runs and fails.
The ECM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The ECM stores this information in the Freeze Frame/Failure Records.
The ECM inhibits the cruise control.

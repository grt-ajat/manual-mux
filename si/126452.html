Glow control system check (4JJ1)

1. Glow control system check description of function

The glow control system consists of the ECM, glow relay, and glow plug. This system is activated to assist engine starting when the engine water temperature is low. The ECM turns ON the glow relay for a specified time with the ignition switch turned ON and the engine turned OFF. During after glow, the glow plug works for a specified time even during the engine operation.

Note

  • Glow control operation

The glow control system is activated for 0.5 seconds to 7 seconds according to the engine coolant temperature.
The pre-glow control system is activated at a temperature of 30°C (86°F) or less.
After glow is activated at a temperature of 60°C (140°F) or less.

2. Glow control system check

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JJ1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Prioritized DTC

If DTCs P0117, P0118, P0380, P0685, or P0687 are set, go to the applicable DTC diagnosis.

3. Circuit diagnosis

Verify that all glow plug power supply terminals and all glow plugs are securely fastened, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the power supply terminal and the body ground.

Perform the Glow Relay Control using a scan tool.

Command the relay ON and OFF while observing the test lamp.

Note

  • The test lamp flashes only when an ON command is executed.

If the test lamp operates properly, go to Inspection of glow plug.

If the test lamp does not operate properly, go to Inspection of glow relay.

4. Inspection of glow plug

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the metallic bus bar from the glow plug.

Measure the resistance of each glow plug between glow plug terminals and the body ground, and record the measured values.

Note

  • Measurement should be conducted quickly so that the engine temperature does not change during measurement.

Observe the difference between each measured value.

Standard： 1.0 Ω

If all of the differences are within the specified value, the system is normal.

If the difference is outside the specified range, replace the applicable glow plug. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) glow plug removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) glow plug installation".

5. Inspection of glow relay

Replace the glow relay with a starter relay or a known good relay.

Connect a test lamp between the power supply terminal and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Perform the Glow Relay Control using a scan tool.

Command the relay ON while observing the test lamp.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection of glow relay connector.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of fuse.

6. Inspection of fuse

Inspect the GLOW 60 A slow blow fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the slow blow fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If the fuse is normal, go to Inspection of glow relay switch power supply circuit.

7. Inspection of glow relay switch power supply circuit

Remove the glow relay.

Connect a test lamp between the switch power supply circuit and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection of glow plug power supply circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair an open circuit or high resistance in the glow relay switch power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

8. Inspection of glow plug power supply circuit

Connect a test lamp between the glow plug power supply circuit and the body ground in the glow relay connector.

If the test lamp illuminates, repair a short to the power supply circuit in the glow plug power supply circuit between the glow relay and the glow plug. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • If the glow plug power supply circuit is shorted to the power supply circuit, the glow plug may be damaged.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair an open circuit or high resistance in the glow plug power supply circuit between the glow relay and the glow plug. Go to Repair verification.

9. Inspection of glow relay connector

Inspect the connection status of the glow relay connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the glow relay connector is normal, replace the glow relay. Go to Repair verification.

10. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts, relays, fuses, and harness connectors.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the power supply terminal and the body ground.

Perform the Glow Relay Control using a scan tool.

Command the relay ON and OFF while observing the test lamp.

Note

  • The test lamp flashes only when an ON command is executed.

If the test lamp operates properly, go to Inspection of glow plug.

If the test lamp does not operate properly, go to Prioritized DTC.

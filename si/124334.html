DTC B1120 Push Start Switch (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. DTC B1120 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "229.Description Security and Locks Control 42C.Functional Inspection(Passive Entry and Start System) diagnostic system check-passive entry and start system".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the engine start/stop button.

Open and close the driver-side door.

Observe the Start Switch 1 and 2 displays on the scan tool.

Note

  • After opening or closing the door, perform the operation within 50 seconds.

If both displays show OFF, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the Start Switch 1 display shows ON, go to Inspection for short circuit in engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit.

If the Start Switch 2 display shows ON, go to Inspection for short circuit in engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a fused jumper wire to the engine start/stop button harness connector engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit.

Observe the Start Switch 1 display on the scan tool.

Connect a fused jumper wire to the engine start/stop button harness connector engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit.

Observe the Start Switch 2 display on the scan tool.

If the Start Switch 1 display shows OFF, go to Inspection for open circuit in engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit.

If the Start Switch 2 display shows OFF, go to Inspection for open circuit in engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit.

If both displays show ON, replace the engine start/stop button. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9C.Electrical Control, Entertainment(All models) engine start/stop button removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9C.Electrical Control, Entertainment(All models) engine start/stop button installation".

4. Inspection for short circuit in engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit

Inspect the engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit between the PESS control unit and the engine start/stop button for a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

5. Inspection for short circuit in engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit

Inspect the engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit between the PESS control unit and the engine start/stop button for a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

6. Inspection for open circuit in engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit

Inspect the engine start/stop switch 1 signal circuit between the PESS control unit and the engine start/stop button for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect the engine start/stop switch 1 ground circuit for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit harness connector inspection.

7. Inspection for open circuit in engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit

Inspect the engine start/stop switch 2 signal circuit between the PESS control unit and the engine start/stop button for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect the engine start/stop switch 2 ground circuit for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit harness connector inspection.

8. PESS control unit harness connector inspection

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the PESS control unit harness connector.

Inspect the connection status of the PESS control unit harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

9. PESS control unit replacement

Replace the PESS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit setting".

2. DTC B1120 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Press the engine start/stop button 5 times or more.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

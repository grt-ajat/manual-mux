DTC P0828 (Flash code 29) Up and Down Switch Circuit High (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P0828 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Operate the selector lever to the manual shift position.

Check the Manual Mode Switch display with a scan tool.

If ON is displayed, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If OFF is displayed, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the manual mode switch harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Up Shift Switch and Down Shift Switch displays with a scan tool.

If the Up Shift Switch or Down Shift Switch display shows ON, go to Inspection for short circuit in shift switch signal circuit.

If the Up Shift Switch and Down Shift Switch displays show OFF, go to Light assembly replacement.

4. Circuit diagnosis 3

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the TCM harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the manual select switch signal circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Operate the selector lever to the manual shift position.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in manual select switch signal circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in manual select switch signal circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the manual select switch signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of manual mode switch harness connector.

6. Inspection for short circuit in shift switch signal circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the shift switch signal circuit that displays ON, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

7. Inspection of manual mode switch harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the manual mode switch harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Light assembly replacement.

8. Light assembly replacement

Replace the light assembly. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) selector lever assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) selector lever assembly reassembly".

9. Inspection of TCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to TCM replacement.

10. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

2. DTC P0828 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Verify that the Manual Mode Switch display shows ON with a scan tool when operating the selector lever to the manual shift position.

Verify that the Up Shift Switch display shows ON when operating the selector lever to the up shift position, and that the Down Shift Switch display shows ON when operating to the down shift position.

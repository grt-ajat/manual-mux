DTC C0131[5A] (Flash Code 72) Master Cylinder pressure sensor output rise malfunction (ABS/ESC)

1. DTC C0131[5A] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. EHCU replacement

Replace the EHCU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

2. DTC C0131[5A] repair verification

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Start the engine and operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC as specified in the supporting text.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

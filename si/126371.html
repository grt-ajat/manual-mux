DTC P0103 (Flash Code 91) Mass Air Flow Circuit High Input (4JK1)

1. DTC P0103 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the MAF/IAT sensor harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor display on the scan tool.

Standard： 0.1 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the MAF sensor low reference circuit and battery voltage.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection of MAF/IAT sensor harness connector.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in low reference circuit.

4. Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit or a short to the 5 V reference circuit in the MAF sensor signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

5. Inspection for open circuit in low reference circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the MAF sensor low reference circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

6. Inspection of MAF/IAT sensor harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the MAF/IAT sensor harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the MAF/IAT sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1F.Induction(4JK1) MAF and IAT sensor removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1F.Induction(4JK1) MAF and IAT sensor installation".

7. Inspection of ECM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to ECM replacement.

8. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P0103 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

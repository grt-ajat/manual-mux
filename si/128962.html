DTC C0390 Transfer 4WD switch circuit low (All models)

1. DTC C0390 description of DTC

The transfer shift actuator is installed in the transfer assembly. Changeovers between the 2-wheel and 4-wheel drives and between high and low are conducted by the single actuator. The transfer shift actuator has the built-in limit switches 1 to 4. The 2 - 4 switch and the neutral switch are installed in the transfer. The TCCM recognizes the current transfer drive status from a combination of the limit switch 1 to 4, the 2 - 4 switch, and the neutral switch.
The TCCM sets this DTC when it detects that the 2 to 4 switch is ON while the transfer shift actuator operation has been completed and the limit switch is in the 2H status.

2. DTC C0390 condition for setting the DTC

The battery voltage is 10 - 16 V.
The transfer shift actuator operation has been completed.
When a status has been detected for 10 seconds that the 2 - 4 switch is ON while the limit switch is in the 2H status.

3. DTC C0390 action taken when the DTC sets

The TCCM turns on the check 4WD warning light when a failure is detected by performing a diagnosis.
Forcibly drives the axle actuator to the 2-wheel drive side when the transfer shift actuator operation has been completed and the limit switch is in the 2H status.
The TCCM is reset after the ignition switch is turned OFF.

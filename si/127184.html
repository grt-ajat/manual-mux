Mix actuator inspection (All models)

1. Mix actuator inspection

1. Inspect the mix actuator.

Note

  • Turn ON the ignition switch.
  • Using a digital multimeter or oscilloscope, inspect the signal voltage between the mix actuator connector No. 1 and mix actuator connector No. 2.
  • Using an oscilloscope, inspect the signal waveform between the mix actuator terminal No. 3 and mix actuator terminal No. 2.
  • Operate the A/C control switch and inspect the motor rotation.
Instruments set2 V/DIV, 100 ms/DIV
Measurement conditionsFan switch LO

Note

  • Voltage waveform

Note

  • Signal (Pulse waveform)

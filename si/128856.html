Steering column inspection (All models)

1. Steering column inspection

1. Inspect the steering column.

Note

  • When an abnormality is found in the inspection, replace the steering column assembly.
  • Column capsule
  • Check the capsule of the steering column assembly.
  • They should be fit into all the brackets.
  • By pushing and pulling by hand, check for sagging.
  • Confirm that there is no gap between the capsule and the bracket.

  1. There should be no gap.

Note

  • Column universal joint or power steering unit and steering shaft
  • When rotating the joint, if resistance is felt, replace the lower second steering shaft.
  • Shaft universal joint or lower second steering shaft and second steering shaft
  • When rotating the joint, if resistance is felt, replace the second steering shaft assembly.
  • Leaning device

Caution

  • Do not release the tilt lever with the steering column removed.

Note

  • The operation of the leaning device should be smooth.
  • When locking the leaning device, confirm that the steering column is securely latched by pushing the steering wheel up and down.

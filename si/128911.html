DTC U0074[00] (Flash Code 17) Control module communication Bus off - Local CAN (ABS/ESC)

1. DTC U0074[00] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. Inspection of steering angle sensor

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the steering angle sensor harness connector.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, replace the steering angle sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) steering angle sensor removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) steering angle sensor installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) steering angle sensor setting".

If a DTC is set, go to Inspection of yaw rate sensor.

3. Inspection of yaw rate sensor

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the yaw rate sensor harness connector.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, replace the yaw rate sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) yaw rate sensor removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) yaw rate sensor installation".

If a DTC is set, go to Circuit diagnosis.

4. Circuit diagnosis

Inspect the CAN Low circuit and the CAN High circuit for a short to the power supply circuit, a short to ground, or a short together between circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to EHCU replacement.

5. EHCU replacement

Replace the EHCU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

2. DTC U0074[00] repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

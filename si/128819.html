Parking brake cable removal (All models)

1. Console box removal

Note

  • The following applies to the manual transmission.

1. Remove the shift lever knob from the shift lever.

2. Remove the shift console cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to the manual transmission.

  1. Shift lever knob
  2. Shift console cover
  3. Cover (2WD models)
  4. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • The following applies to the automatic transmission.

  1. Shift console cover
  2. Cover (2WD models)
  3. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector.
  • The following applies to the 2WD model.

3. Remove the cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to the 4WD model.

4. Remove the 4WD switch from the console box.

Note

  • Disconnect the 4WD switch connector.

5. Remove the console box from the floor.

Note

  • Remove the parking brake cover from the console box.
  • Remove the 2 clips and the 1 screw.
  • Open the console box cover and remove 2 screws.
  • Remove the control box backward.

  1. Cover (2WD models)
  2. 4WD switch (4WD models)
  3. Screw
  4. Parking brake cover
  5. Console box
  6. Clip

2. Disc wheel removal

1. Remove the disc wheel from vehicle.

3. Disc brake pad removal

1. Remove the lock bolt from the brake caliper assembly.

Note

  • Turn the brake caliper facing upward.

Caution

  • While the caliper is removed from the brake support, never depress the brake pedal to avoid the piston abruptly bouncing out.

2. Remove the disc brake pad from the brake support.

Note

  • Remove it with the shim attached.
  • Put an alignment mark in the pad position as a guide for installation.

3. Remove the clip from the brake support.

Note

  • Dispose of the used pad clip and use a new one.

  1. Pad clip
  2. Inner pad
  3. Seven layered shim
  4. Indicator
  5. Outer pad

Note

  • Completely remove old grease remaining between the clip and brake support.

4. Brake support removal

1. Remove the brake support from the rear axle.

Note

  • Remove the brake caliper and brake support as a set from the rear axle.
  • Secure the remove brake support to the frame using a wire, etc.

Caution

  • Do not depress the brake pedal after removing the brake caliper and brake support.

5. Rear rotor removal

1. Remove the rear rotor from the axle shaft.

6. Brake shoe assembly removal

1. Remove the shoe holding cup from the brake shoe pin.

2. Remove the shoe holding spring from the brake shoe pin.

3. Remove the return spring from the brake shoe assembly.

4. Remove the adjuster spring from the adjuster.

5. Disconnect the parking brake cable from the lever.

6. Remove the strut from the lever.

7. Remove the brake shoe assembly from the support plate.

8. Remove the lever from the brake shoe assembly.

7. Parking brake cable removal

1. Remove the parking brake cable from the back plate.

Note

  • Remove the nut that fastens the parking brake cable to the back plate.

Caution

  • Check the mounting direction of the adjuster.
  • Do not twist or bend the cable.
  • Be careful for damage to the cable, which will cause insufficient operation.

2. Remove the parking brake cable bracket from the lower link.

Note

  • Remove the nut that fastens the cable.

3. Remove the parking brake cable from the frame.

4. Remove the parking brake cable from the parking brake lever.

Note

  • Remove the T-end of the parking brake cable from the equalizer of the front parking brake cable.

5. Remove the retainer from vehicle.

Note

  • Remove the nut, and remove the retainer and parking brake cable from the vehicle.

6. Remove the parking brake cable from the retainer.

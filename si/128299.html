DTC P2720 (Flash code 35) Pressure Control Solenoid C (SLT) Control Circuit Low (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P2720 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the transmission internal harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the pressure control solenoid C (SLT) high side control circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Note

  • If the circuit is normal, the test lamp illuminates, and then turns OFF.

If the test lamp illuminates and turns OFF, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high side control circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the pressure control solenoid C (SLT) high side control circuit and the low side control circuit.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Note

  • If the circuit is normal, the test lamp illuminates, and then turns OFF.

If the test lamp illuminates and turns OFF, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in low side control circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 3

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the TCM harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the pressure control solenoid C (SLT) low side control circuit and the battery power supply.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of transmission internal harness connector.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for short circuit in low side control circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high side control circuit

Inspect the high side control circuit for an open circuit, a high resistance, and a short to ground or the low side control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

6. Inspection for open circuit in low side control circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or a high resistance in the low side control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

7. Inspection for short circuit in low side control circuit

Inspect for a short to ground in the low side control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

8. Inspection of transmission internal harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the transmission internal harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of transmission internal harness.

9. Inspection of transmission internal harness

Inspect the pressure control solenoid C (SLT) circuit of the transmission internal harness for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, replace the valve body. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly reassembly".

Caution

  • After replacing the valve assembly, clear the self-learning data with a scan tool.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly installation".

10. Inspection of TCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to TCM replacement.

11. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

2. DTC P2720 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Note

  • The ECM sets DTC P0700 when the TCM sets emission related DTC(s). Clean the ECM DTC after TCM DTC(s) is cleared.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Start the engine and drive the vehicle in the D range.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

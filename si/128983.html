Brake pedal feeling is abnormal (All models)

1. Brake pedal feeling is abnormal description of symptom

Brake pedal feeling is abnormal.

2. Brake pedal feeling is abnormal diagnostics

1. The brake pedal is depressed.

2. Check the stoplight.

3. If the stoplight lights up, turn the ignition switch OFF.

4. Disconnect the harness connector from the EHCU.

5. Turn ON the ignition switch.

6. The brake pedal is depressed.

7. Measure the voltage between the stoplight switch input signal terminal of the EHCU harness connector and the GND.

Standard： 10 to 16 V

8. If the reading is less than or equal to the specified value, inspect the stoplight switch input signal circuit.

Note

  • There should be no open circuits or high resistance.

9. If the stoplight does not turn on, turn the ignition switch OFF.

10. Inspect the stoplight fuse.

11. If the fuse is an open circuit, replace it.

Note

  • If the fuse blows out again, repair the cause of open circuits.

12. The brake pedal is depressed.

13. Check the stoplight.

14. If the stoplight does not turn on, disconnect the harness connector from the stoplight switch.

15. Connect a test cable with fuse between the terminals of the stoplight switch harness connector.

16. Check the stoplight.

17. If the stoplights, replace the stoplight switch.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) stoplight switch removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) stoplight switch installation".

18. If the stop light does not illuminate, inspect the stoplight circuit.

Note

  • There should be no open circuits or high resistance.

19. Inspect the brake fluid pressure piping.

Note

  • There should be no leak or air mixing.

20. Replace the EHCU.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

21. Set the vehicle information on the EHCU.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

22. Perform 0-point learning of the steering angle sensor.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) steering angle sensor setting".

23. Restore the vehicle.

24. Perform a test-run.

25. Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

Note

  • If a DTC has been detected, inspect the applicable DTC.

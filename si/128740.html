Front differential cage inspection (All models)

1. Front differential cage assembly inspection

Note

  • Make all necessary adjustments, repairs and parts replacements when worn, damage, or other abnormalities are found during the inspection.
  • Visually inspect the following parts for wear, damage or other abnormal conditions.
    Side gear, pinion gear, cross pin
    Differential cage
    Thrust washer

2. Pinion gear inspection

1. Measure the pinion gear using the micrometer.

Note

  • Measure the clearance between the pinion gear and cross pin.

Standard： 0.06 to 0.12 mm { 0.002 to 0.005 in }

Limit： 0.2 mm { 0.008 in }

3. Side gear inspection

1. Measure the side gear using the micrometer.

Note

  • Inspect the clearance between the side gear and differential cage.

Standard： 0.03 to 0.10 mm { 0.001 to 0.004 in }

Limit： 0.15 mm { 0.006 in }

  1. Side gear outer diameter
  2. Differential cage inner diameter

Note

  • Inspect the play of the side gear and axle shaft spline.
  • Measure the play using a dial gauge.

Standard： 0.08 to 0.36 mm { 0.003 to 0.014 in }

Limit： 0.5 mm { 0.02 in }

4. Front differential cage inspection

1. Inspect the differential cage.

Note

  • If any worn, damage, corrosion or other abnormalities are found, repair or replace the defective parts.
  • Inspect and correct any flaws or roughness on the fitting surfaces of the ring gear and side gear, and cross pin hole.
  • For minor scratches and roughness, use an oil stone or fine sandpaper to repair.

Rear cooler unit removal (All models)

1. Refrigerant preparation

1. Collect refrigerant.

Caution

  • For collection of refrigerant, refer to the instruction manual for the refrigerant collector.

2. Battery ground cable disconnect

1. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

3. Rear cooler pipe removal

1. Remove the rear cooler pipe from the rear cooler unit.

Note

  • Remove the 2 rear cooler pipe mounting bolts.
  • After removing the connection for each pipe, put a cap or cover on the connections to prevent foreign material from entering the rear cooler pipe.

  1. Rear cooler pipe
  2. Bolt
  3. Nut

4. Sill plate removal

1. Remove the sill plate from the floor.

Note

  • Remove the front sill plate.

  1. Front sill plate

Note

  • Remove the rear sill plate.

  1. Rear sill plate

5. Rear door finisher removal

1. Remove the rear door finisher from the body.

  1. Rear door finisher

6. Tail gate weather strip removal

1. Remove the tail gate weather strip from vehicle.

  1. Tail gate weather strip

2. Remove the buffer from the body.

Note

  • After removing the weather strip, remove the buffer so as not to damage the painted surfaces.

  1. Buffer
  2. Weather strip

7. Luggage rear trim removal

1. Remove the luggage rear trim from the floor.

  1. Luggage rear trim

8. Lower quarter trim cover removal

1. Remove the accessory socket from the lower quarter trim cover.

Note

  • This procedure is applicable to the vehicle with accessory socket.

2. Remove the luggage hook from the quarter panel.

Note

  • Remove the cover and the screw.

3. Remove the lower quarter trim cover from the quarter panel.

Note

  • Remove the cover with a small flat-blade screwdriver, etc., so as not to damage the lower quarter trim cover, and remove the screw.

  1. Luggage hook
  2. Screw
  3. Cover
  4. Cover
  5. Screw
  6. Lower quarter trim cover

9. Rear cooler unit removal

1. Remove the rear cooler unit from vehicle.

Note

  • Remove a total of 3 nuts (2 under the floor and 1 above the floor).
  • Remove the connection for the harness connector.
  • Remove the lower duct.

  1. Nut
  2. Rear cooler unit
  3. Lower duct
  4. Quarter pillar duct (Not replaceable)

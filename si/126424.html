DTC P2229 (Flash Code 71) Barometric Pressure Circuit High (4JK1)

1. DTC P2229 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the barometric pressure sensor harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Barometric Pressure (BARO) Sensor display on the scan tool.

Standard： 0.1 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value and DTC P0651 is set, go to DTC P0651.

If the reading is less than the specified value and DTC P0651 is not set, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the barometric pressure sensor low reference circuit and battery voltage.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection of barometric pressure sensor harness connector.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in low reference circuit.

4. Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit

Inspect the barometric pressure sensor signal circuit for a short to the power supply circuit or a short to the 5 V reference circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • The barometric pressure sensor may be damaged if the sensor signal circuit is shorted to the power supply.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

5. Inspection for open circuit in low reference circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the barometric pressure sensor low reference circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • The barometric pressure sensor shares the low reference circuit with other sensors. A fault condition in the low reference circuit may set DTCs on sensors that share this circuit.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

6. Inspection of barometric pressure sensor harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the barometric pressure sensor harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the barometric pressure sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) barometric pressure sensor removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) barometric pressure sensor installation".

7. Inspection of ECM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to ECM replacement.

8. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P2229 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

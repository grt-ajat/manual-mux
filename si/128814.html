Rear disc brake disassembly (All models)

1. Brake caliper assembly disassembly

1. Remove the lock pin from the brake caliper assembly.

2. Remove the guide pin from the brake caliper assembly.

Caution

  • Do not remove pin boot, lock pin, or guide pin except when necessary.

3. Remove the pin boot from the brake caliper assembly.

  1. Lock pin
  2. Guide pin
  3. Pin boot

4. Remove the boot from the brake caliper assembly.

Note

  • Remove the piston boot.

Caution

  • Do not reuse the piston boot.

5. Remove the piston from the brake caliper assembly.

Note

  • Sandwich a piece of wood in the caliper, blow air into the brake pipe mounting hole, and remove the piston.

6. Remove the piston seal from the piston.

Front seat removal (All models)

1. SRS control unit preparation

1. Deactivate the SRS function.

Note

  • Turn the ignition switch to LOCK and remove the key.
  • Remove the SRS fuse from the fuse relay box or disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

2. Front seat removal

1. Remove the front seat from the floor panel.

Note

  • Remove the rear cover.
  • Remove the 4 bolts.

  1. Front seat
  2. Rear cover

Note

  • Disconnect the seat belt switch harness connector.
  • For vehicles with side airbags, disconnect the side airbag harness connector.
  • For vehicles with power seat, disconnect the power seat harness connector.

  1. Seat belt switch harness connector
  2. Side airbag harness connector
  3. Power seat harness connector

3. Buckle side driver seat belt removal

1. Remove the driver seat belt buckle from the driver seat.

Note

  • Remove the driver seat belt buckle mounting bolt.

  1. Driver seat belt buckle

4. Buckle side passenger seat belt removal

1. Remove the passenger seat belt buckle from the passenger seat.

Note

  • Remove the passenger seat belt buckle mounting bolt.

  1. Passenger seat belt buckle

5. Front seat back removal

1. Remove the front seat back from the front seat.

Note

  • Remove the headrest.
  • The following applies to bucket seats.
  • Remove the lever knob.
  • Remove the side cover.

  1. Lever knob
  2. Side cover
  3. Side cover

Note

  • The following applies to seats with a seat lifter.
  • Remove the lever knob.
  • Remove the cap and lifter lever.
  • Remove the side cover.

  1. Cap
  2. Lever knob
  3. Side cover
  4. Side cover
  5. Lifter lever

Note

  • The following applies to power seats.
  • Remove the switch knob.
  • Remove the side cover.

  1. Side cover
  2. Side cover
  3. Switch knob

Note

  • Remove the J hook of the seat back trim.
  • Remove the 4 bolts, and remove the seat back.

6. Seat slide adjuster removal

1. Remove the seat slide adjuster from the front seat.

Note

  • Remove the 4 fixing nuts inside the slide adjuster.
  • Remove the 3 fixing nuts outside the slide adjuster.

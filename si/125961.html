Fuel tank unit removal (4JJ1)

1. Battery ground cable disconnect

1. Open the engine hood assembly.

2. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

3. Raise vehicle using the jack.

2. Fuel filler cap removal

1. Remove the fuel filler cap from the filler neck.

Note

  • Gradually loosen the fuel filler cap.

Caution

  • Be careful with fuel spouts due to a pressure change in the fuel tank.
  • Cover the opening of the filler neck to prevent foreign material from getting inside.

3. Fuel tube disconnect

1. Disconnect the fuel feed tube from the fuel hose.

2. Disconnect the fuel return tube from the fuel hose.

  1. Fuel feed tube
  2. Fuel return tube

4. Fuel tank removal

1. Disconnect the breather hose from the filler neck.

2. Disconnect the fuel filler hose from the filler neck.

3. Disconnect the evaporator hose from the filler neck.

Note

  • Remove the clip and disconnect the breather hose, filler hose, and evaporator hose.

  1. Fuel filler hose
  2. Evaporator hose
  3. Breather hose

4. Hold the fuel tank using the lifter.

Note

  • Support the bottom of the fuel tank using the lifter.

5. Remove the fuel tank band from the frame.

6. Disconnect the connector from the fuel tank unit.

7. Remove the fuel tank from vehicle.

Note

  • Lower the lifter and remove the fuel tank from the vehicle.

Caution

  • Do not damage the hose and the tube through contact with other parts.

5. Fuel tank unit removal

1. Remove the fuel tube from the fuel tank unit.

2. Remove the fuel return tube from the fuel tank unit.

3. Remove the retainer ring from the fuel tank.

Note

  • Remove it gradually paying attention not to distort the float arm.


SST: 5-8840-2990-0 - wrench; fuel pump retainer

Caution

  • Cover the retainer ring with a shop cloth, etc.
  • Take care as breakage may occur if the special tool is applied to the ribbed bottom portion of the retainer ring.
  • Dispose of the fuel tank unit seal because it cannot be reused.

4. Remove the fuel tank unit from the fuel tank.

Note

  • Remove it gradually paying attention not to distort the float arm.

Caution

  • Cover the opening of the fuel tank unit to prevent foreign material from entering.

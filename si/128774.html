Shift on the fly system inspection (All models)

1. Shift on the fly system inspection

Note

  • Inspect the parts removed.
  • Take a corrective action or replace it if there is worn or damage.

2. Inner axle shaft inspection

1. Inspect the inner axle shaft.

Note

  • Inspect and check the inner axle shaft for abnormal conditions such as worn and damage.

Note

  • Check the spline section for twisting, worn and cracks.
  • If there is any abnormality, replace the shaft with new one.
  • If there is any abnormality in gears including the slide section of the sleeve, replace the shaft with new one.

2. Inspect the inner axle shaft using a dial gauge.

Note

  • Support the center of both ends of the inner axle shaft, and measure the fluctuation by slowly turning the shaft using a dial gauge.

Limit： 0.5 mm { 0.02 in }

Note

  • Do not heat to repair any bent of the shaft.

  1. 230 mm {9.06 in}

3. Inner shaft bearing inspection

1. Inspect the bearing.

Note

  • Insert the clutch gear into the axle shaft to inspect the inner shaft bearing conditions.
  • If any abnormality such as nonsmooth bearing movement is observed, replace the bearing with new one.

  1. Clutch gear
  2. Inner shaft bearing
  3. Inner axle shaft

4. Sleeve inspection

1. Inspect the sleeve.

Note

  • Visually inspect the sleeve for run-out, damage or cracks.
  • As they are important components, thoroughly inspect the groove and inner gear.
  • Operate the sleeve coupled with the clutch gear attached to the inner shaft.
  • If any abnormality such as nonsmooth movement is observed, replace the sleeve with new one.
  • Apply gear oil to the gear contact surface.

Note

  • Measure the width of the sleeve center groove.

Limit： 7.1 mm { 0.28 in }

5. Clutch gear inspection

1. Inspect the clatch gear.

Note

  • Visually inspect the clutch gear for any worn, damage, crack or other abnormal conditions.
  • If any abnormality such as nonsmooth movement is observed when it is operated together with the sleeve, replace the clutch gear.
  • At the time of inspection, apply gear oil to the gear contact surface.

Note

  • Measure the outer diameter of the clutch gear.

Limit： 36.98 mm { 1.46 in }

6. Actuator inspection

1. Inspect the actuator.

Note

  • Visually inspect for any worn, damage, crack and other abnormal conditions.
  • Functional inspection: Apply the voltage of 12 V to the terminals 3 and 4 and connect the tester with terminals 1 and 2 to inspect the operations of the actuator according to the table below.

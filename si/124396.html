DTC B2124 Tail Gate LF Antenna malfunction (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. DTC B2124 description of DTC

The external antenna is installed to the rear bumper support. When the tail gate opener switch or tail gate lock request switch is pressed, the external antenna sends signal waves to the electronic key.
If the PESS control unit detects a malfunction in the external antenna circuit, the DTC is set.

2. DTC B2124 condition for setting the DTC

The PESS control unit detects a malfunction in the external antenna circuit 5 consecutive times.

3. DTC B2124 action taken when the DTC sets

When the engine start/stop button is pressed:
The engine start/stop button indicator light flashes for 10 seconds.
"CHECK SYSTEM" will be displayed on the multi-information display for 10 seconds.
The buzzer built into the instrument panel cluster will sound 3 times.

When the door locks are commanded to lock or unlock:
The engine start/stop button indicator light flashes for 10 seconds.
"CHECK SYSTEM" will be displayed on the multi-information display for 10 seconds.
The external buzzer will sound for 3 seconds.

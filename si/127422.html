Washer motor removal (All models)

1. Battery ground cable disconnect

1. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

2. Tank joint hose removal

1. Disconnect the washer hose from the clip.

2. Remove the clip from the tank joint hose.

3. Remove the bolt from the tank joint hose.

4. Remove the tank joint hose from the washer tank.

  1. Connection of washer hose
  2. Clip
  3. Bolt

3. Mudguard removal

1. Remove the mud guard from the front fender panel.

4. Inner liner removal

1. Remove the inner liner from the front fender panel.

Note

  • Remove the fixing screw.

5. Washer tank removal

1. Disconnect the harness connector from the washer motor.

Note

  • Disconnect the windshield wiper from the washer motor.

  1. Washer motor
  2. Washer tank
  3. Harness connector

2. Remove the bolt from the washer tank.

3. Remove the washer tank from vehicle.

Note

  • Remove the top and bottom tabs by tilting the washer tank, and remove the washer tank from the vehicle.

  1. Upper side tab
  2. Washer tank
  3. Bolt
  4. Lower side tab

Note

  • The following applies to models with a rear wiper.
  • Disconnect the washer hose from the washer motor.
  • Disconnect the connector from the washer motor of the rear wiper.

  1. Connector

6. Washer motor removal

1. Remove the washer motor from the washer tank.

Note

  • The following applies to models without a rear wiper.
  • Remove the washer motor of the windshield wiper.

  1. Washer motor

Note

  • The following applies to models with a rear wiper.
  • Remove the washer motor of the rear wiper.

  1. Washer motor, rear wiper side
  2. Washer motor, windshield wiper side

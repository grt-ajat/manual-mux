Tire air pressure inspection (All models)

1. Tire air pressure inspection description of function

The air pressure increases by approximately 15% when the tires are warmed up while driving, therefore inspect the air pressure when the tires are cool.

Inspection conditions for tire air pressure
The tires are cool.
3 hours or longer has passed since the vehicle was stopped.
The tires have been driven within 1.6 km {1 mile}.

Standard air pressure confirmation
The tire air pressure specification label is attached to the inside of the door opening.
There may be some other tire sizes described on the label in addition to the size of tires that are installed, so make sure to confirm the size of the tires that are currently installed.

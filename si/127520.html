Caution

  • Make sure to use a liquid gasket with the above product name or equivalent.
  • Use an appropriate amount of liquid gasket.
  • Follow the handling precautions for the product.
  • Do not use LOCTITE 515, 518 or FMD 127, as they are anaerobic, and do not provide sufficient effect when there is a gap larger than 0.25 mm (0.001 in) between the contact surfaces of metals.

Application method
Wipe any water and oil from the contact surface.
Apply liquid gasket of the specified bead width to one side of the contact surface. Make sure there are no cuts in the beads.

Caution

  • Make sure the contact surface is dry.

Note

  • If the repair manual specifies an application method, follow that method.

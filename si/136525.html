DTC B0021[0D] (Flash Code 83) Side Airbag (Right) Circuit High Resistance (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B0021[0D] description of DTC

When the ignition switch is turned ON, the SRS control unit performs a test that diagnoses crucial malfunctions in the SRS control unit. When it passes these tests, the voltage is measured to verify that the activation circuit voltage is within the normal voltage range.
After that, the SRS control unit performs the resistance measurement test. A minute current of the specified amount is sent to between the Low and High terminals of the right-side side airbag. The SRS control unit calculates the difference in the voltage between the Low and High terminals of the right-side side airbag to calculate the composite resistance of the High and Low circuits of the right-side side airbag and the connector terminal contact point.

2. DTC B0021[0D] condition for setting the DTC

A resistance of 7 Ω or more is detected in the right side airbag circuit for at least 4 seconds.

3. DTC B0021[0D] action taken when the DTC sets

The SRS control unit illuminates the SRS airbag warning light.

Door open warning light Inoperative (BCM with PESS)

1. Door open warning light Inoperative description of function

If the door or tailgate is opened with the ignition switch in the ON position, the door open warning light illuminates and will turn OFF when the door or tailgate is closed. While the BCM recognizes that the door is open based on door switch and tailgate switch input, the door open warning light illuminates by sending a lamp illumination signal to the instrument panel cluster.

2. Door open warning light Inoperative

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "223.Description Body Control 36C.Functional Inspection(BCM with PESS) diagnostic system check-body controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Open all doors and the tailgate.

Observe the Door Switch display on the scan tool.

If OPEN is displayed, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

If CLOSE is displayed, go to Door switch and tailgate switch inspection.

3. Door switch and tailgate switch inspection

Inspect the door switch and the tailgate switch, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) door switch inspection".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) door switch removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) door switch installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) tail gate lock removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) tail gate lock installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) tail gate lock inspection".

If it is normal, go to Inspection for open circuit in door switch and tailgate switch signal circuit.

4. Inspection for open circuit in door switch and tailgate switch signal circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the door switch and tailgate switch signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of BCM harness connector.

5. Circuit diagnosis 3

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the BCM harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the door open warning light turns OFF, go to Circuit diagnosis 4.

If the door open warning light illuminates, repair a short to ground in the door open warning light control circuit. Go to Repair verification.

6. Circuit diagnosis 4

Remove the METER 10 A fuse.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the door open warning light control circuit of the BCM harness connector and the body ground.

Standard： 1 V

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis 5.

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, repair a short to the power supply circuit in the door open warning light control circuit. Go to Repair verification.

7. Circuit diagnosis 5

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Install the METER 10 A fuse.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a fused jumper wire between the door open warning light control circuit of the BCM harness connector and the body ground.

If the door open warning light does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in door open warning light control circuit.

If the door open warning light illuminates, go to Inspection of BCM harness connector.

8. Inspection for open circuit in door open warning light control circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the door open warning light control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector.

9. Inspection of instrument panel cluster harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the instrument panel cluster harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the instrument panel cluster. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster setting".

10. Inspection of BCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the BCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to BCM replacement.

11. BCM replacement

Replace the BCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM setting".

12. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected fuses and harness connectors.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Open all doors and the tailgate.

If the door open warning light does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

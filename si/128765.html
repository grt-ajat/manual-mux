Drive shaft assembly disassembly (All models)

1. Drive shaft assembly disassembly

Note

  • Disassemble the right side by following the same procedure as that for disassembly of the left side.

1. Remove the boot band from the drive shaft.

Note

  • Release 3 tabs of large and small boot bands on the DOJ side using a hammer and chisel.

Caution

  • Be careful not to damage bellows when removing the bands.

  1. Boot band

2. Remove the circlip from the drive shaft.

Note

  • Force it open using a driver or equivalent.

  1. Circlip

3. Disassemble the drive shaft.

Note

  • Remove 6 balls using a driver or the like.

  1. Ball

Note

  • Use snap ring pliers to remove a snap ring that fixes the ball retainer to the center shaft.
  • Remove the ball retainer, ball guide and bellows.

  1. Snap ring

Note

  • Release 3 tabs of large and small boot bands on the DOJ side using a hammer and chisel.

Caution

  • Be careful not to damage bellows when removing the bands.

  1. Boot band

Note

  • Remove the band.
  • Remove the bellows.
  • Remove a dust seal from UJ.
  • Remove the UJ shaft assembly.

2. Axle mounting bracket disassembly

1. Remove the axle mounting bracket from the axle case.

Note

  • Unfasten the mounting bracket fixing bolt to remove the DOJ case assembly from the axle case.
  • Remove the snap ring and bearing.
  • Remove the snap ring and oil seal.
  • Remove the bracket.

2. Remove the bushing from the bracket using special tool.

Note

  • Use a remover and hammer for removal of the bushing.

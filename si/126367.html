DTC P0091 (Flash Code 247) Fuel Pressure Regulator Control Circuit Low (4JK1)

1. DTC P0091 diagnostics

Note

  • If the suction control valve low side circuit is shorted to ground, this DTC will not be set. It will cause the engine to stall or not to start.

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. DTC verification

Observe the DTC information for DTC P0092.with a scan tool.

If it is set, diagnose DTC P0092 first, and go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the suction control valve harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the suction control valve low side circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection 1 for short circuit in low side circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the suction control valve high side circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high side circuit.

5. Circuit diagnosis 3

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the suction control valve low side circuit and battery voltage.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 4.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in low side circuit.

6. Circuit diagnosis 4

With the test lamp connected, turn OFF the ignition switch

Disconnect the ECM harness connector.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection 2 for short circuit in low side circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of short circuit in suction control valve circuit.

7. Inspection 1 for short circuit in low side circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the suction control valve low side circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • The ECM may be damaged if the suction control valve low side circuit is shorted to the power supply.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

8. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high side circuit

Inspect the suction control valve high side circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

9. Inspection for open circuit in low side circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the suction control valve low side circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

10. Inspection 2 for short circuit in low side circuit

Inspect for a short to ground in the suction control valve low side circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

11. Inspection for short circuit in suction control valve circuit

Inspect for a short circuit between the suction control valve circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of suction control valve harness connector.

12. Inspection of suction control valve harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the suction control valve harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the suction control valve. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • When the suction control valve is replaced, perform the learning of the fuel supply pumps for the ECM.

Refer to "1.Engine 1C.Fuel System(4JK1) FRP regulator removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1C.Fuel System(4JK1) FRP regulator installation".

13. Inspection of ECM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to ECM replacement.

14. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P0091 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

DTC P2146 (Flash Code 158) Fuel Injector Group 1 Supply Voltage Circuit (4JK1)

1. DTC P2146 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the cylinder No. 1 and No. 4 injector harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the cylinder No. 1 solenoid control circuit and the body ground.

Measure the voltage between the cylinder No. 4 solenoid control circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 10.0 V

If both voltages are more than or equal to the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If any voltage is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in control circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between charge voltage circuit 1 and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection 1 for short circuit in charge voltage circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

4. Circuit diagnosis 3

Connect a test lamp between charge voltage circuit 1 and battery voltage.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection 2 for short circuit in charge voltage circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 4.

5. Circuit diagnosis 4

Measure the insulation resistance between each cylinder No. 1 and No. 4 injector terminal and the body ground.

Standard： 1 MΩ

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in charge voltage circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Cylinder No. 1 and No. 4 injector replacement.

6. Inspection for open circuit in control circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the cylinder No. 1 and No. 4 injector control circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

7. Inspection 1 for short circuit in charge voltage circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in charge voltage circuit 1, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

8. Inspection 2 for short circuit in charge voltage circuit

Inspect for a short to ground in charge voltage circuit 1, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

9. Inspection for open circuit in charge voltage circuit

Inspect the connection status of charge voltage circuit 1 at the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in charge voltage circuit 1, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

10. Injector replacement

Replace the injector. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • When the injector has been replace, set the injector ID code on the ECM.

Refer to "1.Engine 1C.Fuel System(4JK1) injector removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1C.Fuel System(4JK1) injector installation".

11. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P2146 repair verification

Clear the DTCs with the scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for more than 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

Limited slip differential disassembly (All models)

1. Differential cage assembly disassembly

1. Disassemble the differential cage assembly.

Note

  • Fix the side gear holder with a vise, and install the differential cage assembly in place.


SST: 5-8840-2682-0 - side gear holder

  1. Side gear holder

Note

  • Place alignment marks on differential cages A and B.
  • Press down and hold differential cage B against differential cage A.
  • Evenly loosen the 4 screws fixing differential cages A and B to remove them.

Note

  • Disassemble differential cage A and B.

2. Side gear thrust washer removal

1. Remove the side gear thrust washer from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the thrust washer and 2 spring plates.

3. Friction plate removal

1. Remove the friction plate from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the friction plate and friction disc.

4. Pressure ring removal

1. Remove the pressure ring from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the pressure ring and side gear.

5. Pinion gear removal

1. Remove the pinion gear from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the pinion gear and pinion shaft.

6. Side gear removal

1. Remove the side gear from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the side gear and pressure ring.

7. Friction disc removal

1. Remove the friction disc from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the friction disc and friction plate.

8. Spring plate removal

1. Remove the spring plate from the differential cage.

Note

  • Remove the 2 spring plates and thrust washer.

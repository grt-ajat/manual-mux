DTC B1325[03] (Flash Code 63) Battery Voltage too Low (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B1325[03] diagnostics

Note

  • A deteriorated battery may cause this DTC to be set.

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "209.Description Restraints 22C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-SRS controls".

2. Inspection of ignition voltage

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Check the Ignition Voltage display on the scan tool.

Standard： 8.4 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to SRS control unit replacement.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection of charging system.

3. Inspection of charging system

Inspect the charging system, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JJ1) charging system check".

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) charging system check".

If the charging system is normal, go to Inspection of SRS control unit harness connector.

4. Inspection of SRS control unit harness connector

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 15 seconds.

Inspect the ignition power supply terminal for poor connections or corrosion.

If a failure is found in the harness connector, repair or replace as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the harness connector is normal, go to Inspection of SRS control unit ground circuit.

5. Inspection of SRS control unit ground circuit

Inspect the ground circuit for an open circuit or high resistance.

Inspect the ground terminal for poor connections or corrosion.

If a failure is found in the ground circuit or the terminal, repair or replace as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the ground circuit or the terminal is normal, go to SRS control unit replacement.

6. SRS control unit replacement

Replace the SRS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit installation".

2. DTC B1325[03] repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts and harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If the DTC is resolved, clear the DTC with a scan tool.

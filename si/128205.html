Front case disassembly (MUX)

1. Reverse idle shaft bolt removal

1. Remove the reverse idle shaft bolt from the front case.

Note

  • Remove the reverse idle shaft bolt and the gasket.

2. Reverse idle gear shaft removal

1. Remove the reverse shaft bracket from the reverse idle gear shaft.

2. Remove the reverse idle gear from the reverse idle gear shaft.

3. Remove the reverse idle gear shaft from the front case.

Note

  • Remove using the straight pin.

3. Front oil seal removal

1. Remove the front oil seal from the front case using special tool.

Note

  • Use a pilot bearing installer and grip.
  • Remove the front oil seal together with the input shaft needle bearing as a unit.


SST: 5-8840-0007-0 - grip


SST: 5-8840-0125-0 - pilot bearing installer

  2. Pilot bearing installer

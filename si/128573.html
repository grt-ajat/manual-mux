Antitheft indicator light inoperative (BCM with Keyless Entry)

1. Antitheft indicator light inoperative description of function

The anti-theft indication light illuminates for approximately 10 seconds while the BCM checks if the conditions of anti-theft system operation are met. Then, if the operation conditions are met, the light starts flashing and the anti-theft system is activated.
If the conditions of anti-theft system operation are not met because the engine hood is open or for other reasons, the light turns OFF after flashing.

2. Antitheft indicator light inoperative

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "223.Description Body Control 36C.Functional Inspection(BCM with Keyless Entry) diagnostic system check-body controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the anti-theft indication light.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) anti theft horn removal".

Connect a test lamp between the anti-theft indicator control circuit of the anti-theft indication light harness connector and the body ground.

Activate the anti-theft function.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in anti-theft indicator circuit.

If the test lamp flashes after illuminating for 10 seconds, go to Inspection of anti-theft indication light harness connector.

3. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in anti-theft indicator circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the anti-theft indicator ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect the anti-theft indicator control circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of BCM harness connector.

4. Inspection of anti-theft indication light harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the anti-theft indication light harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the anti-theft indication light. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) anti theft horn removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) anti theft horn installation".

5. Inspection of BCM harness connector

Inspect the insulation status of the BCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to BCM replacement.

6. BCM replacement

Replace the BCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM setting".

7. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected harness connectors.

Activate the anti-theft function.

Verify that the anti-theft indication light flashes after illuminating for 10 seconds.

If it does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

Vehicle identification plate
The vehicle identification plate is attached to the vehicle. The vehicle identification plate contains the following information.
VIN
Paint information
Three-digit option code which means production options or special equipment installed on the vehicle at the factory.
Refer to the vehicle identification plate when ordering replacement parts.

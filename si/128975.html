Check ABS, ESC, brake system warning light always on (All models)

1. Check ABS, ESC, brake system warning light always on description of function

The brake system, ABS, and ESC warning lights illuminate when the ignition switch is turned ON, and turn OFF afterwards. When the EHCU detects the ignition switch ON signal, it illuminates the brake system, ABS, and ESC warning lights for 3 seconds for lighting check. The EHCU performs the subsequent control of each lamp by sending lamp operation requests to the instrument panel cluster via CAN communication.

Function of ABS, ESC, and brake system warning lights
The brake system warning light, the ABS warning light, and the ESC warning light inform the driver that a malfunction has occurred and the vehicle needs to be inspected and repaired as soon as possible.
When the brake system warning light, the ABS warning light, and the ESC warning light remain ON in response to the instruction from the EHCU, a DTC is set.

Lighting conditions of ABS, ESC, and brake system warning lights
The warning light illuminates for 3 seconds after the ignition switch is turned ON.
When the EHCU detects a malfunction, the warning light remains ON.

2. Check ABS, ESC, brake system warning light always on

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. DTC verification

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is set, perform the applicable diagnosis for the DTC.

If a DTC is not set, go to Instrument panel cluster replacement.

3. Instrument panel cluster replacement

Replace the instrument panel cluster. After replacing the instrument panel cluster, conduct the setting of the instrument panel cluster.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) instrument panel cluster setting".

4. Repair verification

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Verify that he ABS and ESC warning lights illuminate for approx. 3 seconds and then turn OFF.

Release the parking brake.

Verify that the brake system warning light turns OFF.

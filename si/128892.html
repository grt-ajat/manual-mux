DTC C0161[00] (Flash Code 19) Brake switch circuit malfunction(ABS/ESC) (ABS/ESC)

1. DTC C0161[00] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. Verification of the brake switch signal and the master cylinder pressure sensor signal

Refer to the following table and observe the Master Cylinder Pressure Sensor Input display and the Brake Switch display on the scan tool.

Brake pedalBrake Switch displayMaster Cylinder Pressure Sensor Input display
OFFOFF： 0.4 to 0.6 V
Depress for the free travelFrom OFF to ON： 0.4 to 0.6 V
Fully depressON： 0.5 to 4.5 V increase

If the Master Cylinder Pressure Sensor Input display is outside the specified range, go to EHCU replacement.

If the Brake Switch display does not show OFF, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

If the Brake Switch display does not show ON, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the stoplight switch harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Brake Switch display on the scan tool.

If OFF is displayed, go to Inspection of stoplight switch.

If ON is displayed, repair the short to the power supply circuit in the stoplight switch signal circuit. Go to Repair verification.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Inspect the STOP LIGHT 15 A fuse, and replace as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the STOP LIGHT 15 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the stoplight switch harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a fused jumper wire between the stoplight switch power supply circuit and the signal circuit, and observe the Brake Switch display with a scan tool.

If ON is displayed, go to Inspection of stoplight switch harness connector.

If OFF is displayed, go to Inspection for open circuit in stoplight switch circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in stoplight switch circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the stoplight switch circuit between the STOP LIGHT 15 A fuse and the EHCU, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of EHCU harness connector.

6. Inspection of stoplight switch harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the stoplight switch harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of stoplight switch.

7. Inspection of stoplight switch

Inspect the installation status of the stoplight switch, and adjust as necessary.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) stoplight switch installation".

If it is normal, replace the stoplight switch. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) stoplight switch removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9A.Lighting Systems(All models) stoplight switch installation".

8. Inspection of EHCU harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the EHCU harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to EHCU replacement.

9. EHCU replacement

Replace the EHCU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

2. DTC C0161[00] repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Operate the brake pedal.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

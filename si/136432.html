DTC B0083[39] (Flash Code 36) Front Airbag Sensor (Left) Disconnected (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B0083[39] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "209.Description Restraints 22C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-SRS controls".

2. Inspection of harness connector

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Verify that the harness connector of the left side front airbag sensor circuit is securely connected.

Verify that the harness connector of the left side front airbag sensor circuit is not damaged.

Inspect the harness connector terminal in the left front airbag sensor circuit for corrosion.

If a failure is found in the harness connector, repair or replace as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the harness connector is normal, go to Inspection for open circuit and short to ground in left front airbag sensor circuit.

3. Inspection for open circuit and short to ground in left side front airbag sensor circuit

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 15 seconds.

Inspect the left side front airbag sensor + circuit and - circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection for short to power supply in left front airbag sensor circuit.

4. Inspection for short to power supply in left side front airbag sensor circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the left side front airbag sensor + circuit and - circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Left front airbag sensor replacement.

5. Left side front airbag sensor replacement

Replace the left side front airbag sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) front airbag sensor removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) front airbag sensor installation".

6. DTC verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts and harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is set, go to SRS control unit replacement.

7. SRS control unit replacement

Replace the SRS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit installation".

2. DTC B0083[39] repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts and harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If the DTC is resolved, clear the DTC with a scan tool.

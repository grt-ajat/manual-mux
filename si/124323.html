DTC B1022 Clutch Switch (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. DTC B1022 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "229.Description Security and Locks Control 42C.Functional Inspection(Passive Entry and Start System) diagnostic system check-passive entry and start system".

2. Prioritized DTC

If DTC B1107 is set, go to the DTC B1107 diagnosis.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Clutch Pedal Switch display with a scan tool while operating the clutch pedal.

If the Clutch Pedal Switch display does not change, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the Clutch Pedal Switch display changes, go to Repair verification.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the clutch switch harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Clutch Pedal Switch display on the scan tool while connecting and disconnecting a fused jumper wire between the circuits of the clutch switch harness connector.

If the Clutch Pedal Switch display does not change, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in clutch switch signal circuit.

If the Clutch Pedal Switch display changes, go to Clutch switch harness connector inspection.

5. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in clutch switch signal circuit

Inspect the clutch switch signal circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit harness connector inspection.

6. Clutch switch harness connector inspection

Inspect the connection condition of the clutch switch harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Clutch switch inspection.

7. Clutch switch inspection

Inspect the installation status of the clutch switch, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5E.Clutch System(MUA) clutch control installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5E.Clutch System(MUX) clutch control installation".

If normal, replace the clutch switch. Go to Repair verification.

8. PESS control unit harness connector inspection

Inspect the connection status of the clutch switch input signal circuit at the PESS control unit harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

9. PESS control unit replacement

Replace the PESS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit setting".

2. DTC B1022 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Clutch Pedal Switch display on the scan tool.

Verify that ON is displayed when the clutch pedal is depressed and OFF is displayed when the clutch pedal is released.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

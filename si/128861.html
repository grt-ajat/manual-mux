DTC B2725 Mode Switch Circuit (4WD models)

1. DTC B2725 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "204.Description Driveline, Axle 17C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-transfer case controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the 4WD switch harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a fused jumper wire between the 4WD switch 2H signal circuit of the 4WD switch harness connector and the body ground.

Observe the Mode-Switch 2H-port Status display with a scan tool.

If ON is displayed, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If OFF is displayed, go to Inspection 1 for open circuit in signal circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a fused jumper wire between the 4WD switch 4H signal circuit of the 4WD switch harness connector and the body ground.

Observe the Mode-Switch 4H-port Status display with a scan tool.

If ON is displayed, go to Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit.

If OFF is displayed, go to Inspection 2 for open circuit in signal circuit.

4. Inspection 1 for open circuit in signal circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the 4WD switch 2H signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCCM harness connector.

5. Inspection 2 for open circuit in signal circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the 4WD switch 4H signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCCM harness connector.

6. Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the 4WD switch ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect the installation condition of the ground terminal, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of 4WD switch harness connector.

7. Inspection of 4WD switch harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the 4WD switch harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of 4WD switch.

8. Inspection of 4WD switch

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Remove the 4WD switch.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9C.Electrical Control, Entertainment(All models) 4WD switch removal".

Inspect the 4WD switch, and repair or replace as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9C.Electrical Control, Entertainment(All models) 4WD switch inspection".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9C.Electrical Control, Entertainment(All models) 4WD switch installation".

9. Inspection of TCCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection condition is normal, replace the TCCM. Go to Replacement of TCCM.

10. Replacement of TCCM

Replace the TCCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "3.Driveline, Axle 3D.Transfer Case(All models) TCCM removal".

Refer to "3.Driveline, Axle 3D.Transfer Case(All models) TCCM installation".

2. DTC B2725 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Drive the vehicle in 2H, 4H, and 4L modes respectively.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

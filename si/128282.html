DTC P0722 (Flash code 11) Output Speed Sensor Circuit No Signal (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P0722 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the output speed sensor harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the output speed sensor high circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 2.3 to 2.8 V

If the reading is within the specified range, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the reading is above the specified value, go to Inspection for short circuit in high circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Measure the voltage between the output speed sensor low circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 2.3 to 2.8 V

If the reading is within the specified range, go to Inspection for short circuit in output speed sensor circuit.

If the reading is above the specified value, go to Inspection for short circuit in low circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in low circuit.

4. Inspection for short circuit in high circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the output speed sensor high circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

5. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high circuit

Inspect the output speed sensor high circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

6. Inspection for short circuit in low circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the output speed sensor low circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

7. Inspection of open circuit and short circuit in low circuit

Inspect the output speed sensor low circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

8. Inspection for short circuit in output speed sensor circuit

Inspect for a short together between output speed sensor circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of output speed sensor harness connector.

9. Inspection of output speed sensor harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the output speed sensor harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of output speed sensor.

10. Inspection of output speed sensor

Inspect the output speed sensor for the following conditions, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Sensor damage
Looseness or improper installation of sensor
Excessive gap
Foreign material passing between output speed sensor and parking lock gear
Damage to parking lock gear

If it is normal, replace the output speed sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) output speed sensor removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) output speed sensor installation".

11. Inspection of TCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to TCM replacement.

12. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

2. DTC P0722 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Note

  • The ECM sets DTC P0700 when the TCM sets emission related DTC(s). Clean the ECM DTC after TCM DTC(s) is cleared.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Start the engine and drive the vehicle.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

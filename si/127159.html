Pressure switch removal (All models)

1. Refrigerant preparation

1. Collect refrigerant.

Caution

  • For collection of refrigerant, refer to the instruction manual for the refrigerant collector.

2. Pressure switch removal

1. Remove the power steering oil tank assembly from the power steering oil tank bracket.

Note

  • Lift up only the tank body and let it away without removing the oil tank hose.

2. Disconnect the harness connector from the pressure switch.

3. Remove the pressure switch from the A/C pipe.

Caution

  • Make sure to use a backup wrench when removing the pressure switch.
  • After removing the pressure switch, put a cap or a cover on the connection to prevent any foreign object from getting into the A/C pipe.

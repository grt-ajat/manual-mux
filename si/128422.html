DTC P2762 (Flash code 33) TCC Pressure Control Solenoid (SLU) Control Circuit Performance (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P2762 description of DTC

The torque converter clutch pressure control solenoid (SLU) is installed on the valve body assembly and adjusts the control oil pressure that operates the lock-up clutch. The TCM controls the torque converter clutch pressure control solenoid (SLU) according to the accelerator pedal position, the output shaft rotation, etc. When the pressure control solenoid is activated, the TCM accumulates the difference between the requested pressure control solenoid current and the present pressure control solenoid current as a feedback error current value. The TCM sets a DTC when the accumulation of the torque converter clutch pressure control solenoid (SLU) feedback error current value is too high.

2. DTC P2762 condition for setting the DTC

DTCs P2763 and P2764 are not set.
The battery voltage is between 10.2 - 15.5 V.
The TCM detects that the accumulation of the torque converter clutch pressure control solenoid (SLU) feedback error current value is 12,000 mA or more twice in a row during one driving cycle.

3. DTC P2762 action taken when the DTC sets

Note

  • Euro4 model

When a diagnostic runs and fails, the TCM flashes the check trans warning light.
The TCM requests the MIL to be illuminated when the diagnostic runs and fails.
The TCM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The TCM stores this information in the Failure Records.
The ECM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The ECM stores this information in the Freeze Frame/Failure Records.
The ECM stores DTC P0700 in ECM history at the time the diagnostic fails.
The TCM inhibits the lock-up timing control.
The TCM inhibits the slip lock-up control.
The TCM inhibits the squat control.
The TCM inhibits the self-learning control.
The TCM inhibits the 2nd start control.
When the vehicle speed becomes 10 km/h (6 mph) or lower, the gear position is fixed in the 1st gear.

Note

  • Euro2 and Euro3 model

When a diagnostic runs and fails, the TCM flashes the check trans warning light.
The TCM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The TCM stores this information in the Failure Records.
The TCM inhibits the lock-up timing control.
The TCM inhibits the slip lock-up control.
The TCM inhibits the squat control.
The TCM inhibits the self-learning control.
The TCM inhibits the 2nd start control.
When the vehicle speed becomes 10 km/h (6 mph) or lower, the gear position is fixed in the 1st gear.

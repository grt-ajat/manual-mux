SRS control unit removal (All models)

1. SRS control unit safety information

Caution

  • During the maintenance procedure, take sufficient care in handling the SRS control unit.
  • Take sufficient care not to hit, shake or give impact to the SRS control unit.
  • Applying an impact may cause personal injury due to deployment of airbag or malfunction of SRS.
  • Never turn the ignition switch to ON when the SRS control unit is not properly installed in the vehicle.
  • The SRS control unit malfunction occurs when the ignition switch is turned on with the SRS control unit not properly installed to the vehicle, which may result in injuries due to activation of the airbag.

Warning

  • For the SRS control unit to activate normally, the SRS control unit must be installed to the vehicle properly.
  • Never change the installation position of the SRS control unit and the location of the harness connector .

2. SRS control unit preparation

1. Deactivate the SRS function.

Note

  • Turn the ignition switch to LOCK and remove the key.
  • Remove the SRS fuse from the fuse relay box or disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

3. Console box removal

Note

  • The following applies to manual transmission.

1. Remove the shift lever knob from the shift lever.

2. Remove the shift console cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to the manual transmission.

  1. Shift lever knob
  2. Shift console cover
  3. Cover (2WD models)
  4. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • The following applies to the automatic transmission.

  1. Shift console cover
  2. Cover (2WD models)
  3. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector.
  • The following applies to 2WD.

3. Remove the cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to 4WD.

4. Remove the 4WD switch from the console box.

Note

  • Disconnect the 4WD switch connector.

5. Remove the console box from the floor.

Note

  • Remove the parking brake cover from the console box.
  • Remove the 2 clips and the 1 screw.
  • Open the console box cover and remove 2 screws.
  • Remove the control box backward.

  1. Cover (2WD models)
  2. 4WD switch (4WD models)
  3. Screw
  4. Parking brake cover
  5. Console box
  6. Clip

4. SRS control unit removal

1. Disconnect the harness connector from the SRS control unit.

2. Remove the SRS control unit from the floor.

  1. SRS control unit

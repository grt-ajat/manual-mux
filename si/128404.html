DTC P2720 (Flash code 35) Pressure Control Solenoid C (SLT) Control Circuit Low (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P2720 description of DTC

Pressure control solenoid C (SLT) is installed on the valve body assembly and adjusts the line pressure of the ATF that is discharged from the oil pump based on the control signal from the TCM. When the drive current of pressure control solenoid C (SLT) becomes larger, the line pressure decreases, and when it becomes smaller, the line pressure increases. The TCM detects the energized state of pressure control solenoid C (SLT) based on the voltage value of the high side control circuit and the current value of the low side control circuit of pressure control solenoid C (SLT). When the voltage value and the current value of the pressure control solenoid C (SLT) circuit are too low, the TCM determines that as due to an open in the circuit or a short to ground, and sets a DTC.

2. DTC P2720 condition for setting the DTC

DTCs P2719 and P2721 are not set.
The battery voltage is between 10.2 - 15.5 V.
The TCM detects that the pressure control solenoid C (SLT) low side control circuit voltage is less than 0.018 V.

3. DTC P2720 action taken when the DTC sets

Note

  • Euro4 model

When a diagnostic runs and fails, the TCM flashes the check trans warning light.
The TCM requests the MIL to be illuminated when the diagnostic runs and fails.
The TCM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The TCM stores this information in the Failure Records.
The ECM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The ECM stores this information in the Freeze Frame/Failure Records.
The ECM stores DTC P0700 in ECM history at the time the diagnostic fails.
The TCM inhibits the slope control.
The TCM inhibits the lock-up timing control.
The TCM inhibits the slip lock-up control.
The TCM inhibits the engine torque control.
The TCM inhibits the self-learning control.
The TCM switches the mode to the emergency mode.

Note

  • Euro2 and Euro3 model

When a diagnostic runs and fails, the TCM flashes the check trans warning light.
The TCM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The TCM stores this information in the Failure Records.
The TCM inhibits the slope control.
The TCM inhibits the lock-up timing control.
The TCM inhibits the slip lock-up control.
The TCM inhibits the engine torque control.
The TCM inhibits the self-learning control.
The TCM switches the mode to the emergency mode.

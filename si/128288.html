DTC P2743 (Flash code 27) Transmission Fluid Temperature Sensor 2 Circuit High (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P2743 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the transmission internal harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the transmission fluid temperature sensor 2 signal circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 4.5 to 5.5 V

If the reading is within the specified range, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the reading is above the specified value, go to Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in signal circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Measure the voltage between the transmission fluid temperature sensor 2 signal circuit and the low reference circuit.

Standard： 4.5 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection of transmission internal harness connector.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in low reference circuit.

4. Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the transmission fluid temperature sensor 2 signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

5. Inspection for open circuit in signal circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the transmission fluid temperature sensor 2 signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

6. Inspection for open circuit in low reference circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the transmission fluid temperature sensor 2 low reference circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

7. Inspection of transmission internal harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the transmission internal harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of transmission internal harness.

8. Inspection of transmission internal harness

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the transmission fluid temperature sensor 2 circuit of the transmission internal harness, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, replace the transmission internal harness. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly reassembly".

9. Inspection of TCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to TCM replacement.

10. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

2. DTC P2743 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Note

  • The ECM sets DTC P0700 when the TCM sets emission related DTC(s). Clean the ECM DTC after TCM DTC(s) is cleared.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Start the engine and drive the vehicle for at least 10 minutes.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

Selector lever assembly disassembly (TB-50LS)

1. Selector lever knob removal

1. Remove the selector lever knob from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

Note

  • Remove the 2 screws.

2. Knob cover removal

1. Remove the knob cover from the selector lever knob.

3. Position indicator upper cover removal

1. Remove the position indicator upper cover from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

4. Light assembly removal

1. Remove the light assembly from the position indicator lower cover.

5. Position indicator lower cover removal

1. Remove the position indicator lower cover from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

6. Slide cover A removal

1. Remove the slide cover A from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

7. Slide cover B removal

1. Remove the slide cover B from the slide cover A.

8. Slide cover C removal

1. Remove the slide cover C from the slide cover B.

9. Shift lock release button removal

1. Remove the shift lock release button from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

10. Spring removal

1. Remove the spring from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

11. Shift lock solenoid assembly removal

1. Disconnect the harness connector from the inter lock controller assembly.

2. Remove the shift lock solenoid assembly from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

12. Inter lock controller assembly removal

1. Remove the inter lock controller assembly from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

13. Detent cushion removal

1. Remove the detent cushion from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

Note

  • Remove the detent cushion of base side.

14. Detent cushion removal

1. Remove the detent cushion from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

Note

  • Remove the detent cushion of lever side.

15. Plate spring removal

1. Remove the plate spring from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

16. Seal removal

1. Remove the seal from the shift lever plate sub assembly.

DTC P0697 (Flash Code 57) Sensor Reference Voltage 3 Circuit (4JJ1)

1. DTC P0697 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JJ1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the intake throttle valve harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the intake throttle valve 5 V reference circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 4.7 to 5.3 V

If the reading is within the specified range, replace the intake throttle valve. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1B.Mechanical(4JJ1) intake throttle valve removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1B.Mechanical(4JJ1) intake throttle valve installation".

If the reading is more than the specified value, go to Inspection 1 for short circuit in 5 V reference circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection of boost pressure sensor.

3. Inspection of boost pressure sensor

Connect the DMM to the intake throttle valve harness connector.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the boost pressure sensor harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the voltage display on the DMM.

Standard： 4.7 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, replace the boost pressure sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1F.Induction(4JJ1) boost pressure sensor removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1F.Induction(4JJ1) boost pressure sensor installation".

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection of CKP sensor.

4. Inspection of CKP sensor

Connect the DMM to the intake throttle valve harness connector.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the CKP sensor harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the voltage display on the DMM.

Standard： 4.7 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, replace the CKP sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1B.Mechanical(4JJ1) CKP sensor removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1B.Mechanical(4JJ1) CKP sensor installation".

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection 2 for short circuit in 5 V reference circuit.

5. Inspection 1 for short circuit in 5 V reference circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in 5 V reference circuit 4 between the ECM and the intake throttle valve, the boost pressure sensor, or the CKP sensor, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

6. Inspection 2 for short circuit in 5 V reference circuit

Inspect for a short to ground or a short to the low reference circuit in 5 V reference circuit 4 between the ECM and the intake throttle valve, the boost pressure sensor, or the CKP sensor, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

7. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P0697 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

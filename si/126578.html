Lost communication with the ECM (4JK1)

1. Lost communication with the ECM description of function

The ECM, TCM, TCCM, EHCU, ICU, SRS control unit, BCM, instrument panel cluster, and PESS control unit communicate with the scan tool via the CAN communication bus. The EGR valve, DRM, and auto A/C control unit use the same CAN communication bus when the vehicle is running, but do not use it for communicating with the scan tool.

Note

  • Even if the ECM battery power supply circuit is damaged, the ECM can communicate with the scan tool.

2. Lost communication with the ECM

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Inspection of ECM connector connection

Inspect the connection status of the ECM connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Inspect the ENGINE IG 10 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the ENGINE IG 10 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the ECM harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit of the ECM harness connector and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair the open circuit of ignition power circuit. Go to Repair verification.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the scan tool from the DLC if connected.

Measure the resistance between the CAN Low circuit and the CAN High circuit of the DLC.

Standard： 50 to 70 Ω

Note

  • The combined resistance of the 120 Ω resistor inside the ECM and the 120 Ω resistor inside the instrument panel cluster becomes 60 Ω.

If the reading is outside the specified range, go to Inspection of CAN serial data circuit.

If the reading is within the specified range, go to Inspection of ECM ground circuit.

5. Inspection of ECM ground circuit

Inspect for corrosion or tightness of the ECM ground terminal, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the ECM ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

6. Inspection of CAN serial data circuit

Inspect the connection status of the CAN Low circuit and the CAN High circuit at the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect for poor connections at the joint connector, an open circuit, or high resistance in the CAN Low circuit and the CAN High circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM replacement.

7. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM writing".

8. Repair verification

Verify the communication status with the ECM using a scan tool.

If communication cannot be established, go to Inspection of ECM connector connection.

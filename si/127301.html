Rear door assembly disassembly (All models)

1. Power window switch removal

1. Remove the power window switch from the rear door.

Note

  • Open the cover with a small-sized flat-blade screwdriver, etc., so as not to damage it, and remove the screw.
  • Remove the power window switch, and disconnect the connector.

  1. Power window switch
  2. Screw
  3. Cover

2. Rear door trim panel removal

1. Remove the rear door trim panel from the rear door.

Note

  • Take out the 2 clips for the cover.
  • Remove the screw for the rear door trim panel and take out the 9 clips.

  1. Screw
  2. Clip
  3. Cover

3. Bracket removal

1. Remove the bracket from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 screws.

4. Door speaker removal

1. Remove the door speaker from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the fastening bolt and disconnect the connector.

5. Weather strip removal

1. Remove the weather strip from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the clip.

6. Waterproof sheet removal

1. Remove the waterproof sheet from the rear door.

Note

  • When peeling, take care not to scratch the seat.

7. Inside lever removal

1. Disconnect the inside lever from the door lock cable.

8. Outer waist seal removal

1. Remove the outer waist seal from the rear door.

9. Glassrun rear channel upper removal

1. Remove the glassrun rear channel upper from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 fixing bolts.

10. Fix window assembly removal

1. Remove the fix window assembly from the rear door.

Note

  • Pull the fix window assembly forward.

11. Rear door glass removal

1. Remove the rear door glass from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 bolts, and pull out the rear door glass in an upward direction.

12. Rear door window regulator removal

1. Remove the window regulator from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 4 setting bolts.
  • Disconnect the power window motor connector.

13. Glassrun rear channel lower removal

1. Remove the glassrun rear channel lower from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 fixing bolts.

14. Glass run removal

1. Remove the glass-run from the rear door.

Note

  • Pull it out from the door frame of the channel.

15. Door lock assembly removal

1. Remove the door lock assembly from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 3 setting screws and the bolt.
  • Disconnect the linkage and harness connector.

16. Outside handle removal

1. Remove the outside handle from the rear door.

Note

  • Remove the 2 fixing bolts.

17. Door harness removal

1. Remove the door harness from the rear door.

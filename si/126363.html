DTC P0045 (Flash Code 33) Turbocharger Boost Control Solenoid Circuit (4JK1)

1. DTC P0045 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the turbocharger control solenoid valve harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the turbocharger control solenoid valve high side circuit and the low side circuit.

Perform the Turbocharger Solenoid Control with a scan tool.

Command the solenoid valve to INCREASE and DECREASE while observing the test lamp.

If the brightness of the test lamp changes, go to Turbocharger control solenoid valve harness connector inspection.

If the test lamp does not illuminate or the brightness does not change, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the turbocharger control solenoid valve high side circuit and the body ground.

Perform the Turbocharger Solenoid Control with a scan tool.

Command the solenoid valve INCREASE and DECREASE while observing the test lamp.

If the brightness of the test lamp changes, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

If the test lamp does not illuminate or the brightness of the test lamp does not change, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in High side control circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 3

Connect a test lamp between the turbocharger control solenoid valve low side circuit and the power supply circuit.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for short circuit in low side circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in low side circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in high side circuit

Inspect the turbocharger control solenoid valve high side circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

6. Inspection for short circuit in low side circuit

Inspect the turbocharger control solenoid valve low side circuit for a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM harness connector inspection.

7. Inspection for open circuit in low side circuit

Inspect the turbocharger control solenoid valve low side circuit for an open circuit or high resistance, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to ECM harness connector inspection.

8. Inspection of turbocharger control solenoid valve　harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the turbocharger control solenoid valve harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the turbocharger control solenoid valve. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1F.Induction(4JK1) turbocharger control solenoid removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1F.Induction(4JK1) turbocharger control solenoid installation".

9. Inspection of ECM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to ECM replacement.

10. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JK1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P0045 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

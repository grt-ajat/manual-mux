DTC U010A (Flash Code 346) Lost Communication with Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Control Module (4JK1)

1. DTC U010A diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Inspection of scan tool communication

Check if communication with the ECM is available using a scan tool.

If a communication condition exists, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

If communication cannot be established, refer to Lost Communication With The ECM, and repair as necessary.

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JK1) lost communication with the ECM".

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the EGR valve harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Connect a test lamp between the power supply circuit and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in power supply circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the power supply circuit and the ground circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in CAN circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair an open circuit or high resistance in the ground circuit. Go to Repair verification.

5. Inspection for open circuit in power supply circuit

Inspect the ENGINE 10 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the ENGINE 10 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • The EGR valve shares the power supply circuit with other components. The ENGINE 10 A fuse blowout may set DTCs on components that share this circuit.

If the fuse is normal, repair an open circuit or high resistance in the power supply circuit between the ENGINE 10 A fuse and the EGR valve. Go to Repair verification.

6. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in CAN circuit

Inspect the CAN circuit between the ECM and the EGR valve for an open circuit, high resistance, a short to the power supply circuit or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If it is normal, go to Inspection of EGR valve harness connector.

7. Inspection of EGR valve harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the EGR valve harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, replace the EGR valve. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1H.Aux. Emission Control Devices(4JK1) EGR valve removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1H.Aux. Emission Control Devices(4JK1) EGR valve installation".

2. DTC U010A repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn the ignition switch OFF for 30 seconds or longer.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

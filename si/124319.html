DTC B1011 Parking/Neutral Detection Switch (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. DTC B1011 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "229.Description Security and Locks Control 42C.Functional Inspection(Passive Entry and Start System) diagnostic system check-passive entry and start system".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Parking/Neutral Detection display on the scan tool while operating the shift lever.

If the Parking/Neutral Detection display does not change, go to Parking circuit diagnosis 2.

If the Parking/Neutral Detection display changes, go to Repair verification.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the neutral switch harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the Parking/Neutral Detection display on the scan tool while connecting and disconnecting a fused jumper wire between the circuits of the neutral switch harness connector.

If the Parking/Neutral Detection display does not change, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in neutral switch power supply circuit.

If the Parking/Neutral Detection display changes, go to Neutral switch harness connector inspection.

4. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in neutral switch power supply circuit

Inspect the neutral switch power supply circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in neutral switch signal circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in neutral switch signal circuit

Inspect the neutral switch signal circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to PESS control unit harness connector inspection.

6. Neutral switch harness connector inspection

Inspect the connection status of the neutral switch harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Neutral switch inspection.

7. Neutral switch inspection.

Inspect the installation status of the neutral switch, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If normal, replace the neutral switch. Go to Repair verification.

8. PESS control unit harness connector inspection

Inspect the connection status of the neutral switch signal circuit at the PESS control unit harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to PESS control unit replacement.

9. PESS control unit replacement

Replace the PESS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9J.Security and Locks(All models) PESS control unit setting".

2. DTC B1011 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

Abnormal noise and vibration from axle (All models)

1. Abnormal noise and vibration from axle diagnostics

Note

  • Abnormal sound from the front axle

Lack of gear oil.
Refill the gear oil.

Wrong gear oil or gear oil degradation.
Replace the gear oil.

Excessive or insufficient backlash between the drive pinion and ring gear.
Adjust the backlash.

Looseness of the drive pinion bearing.
Adjust the drive pinion bearing preload.

Worn of the drive pinion bearing.
Replace the drive pinion bearing.

Looseness of the differential side bearing.
Adjust the differential side bearing preload.

Worn of the differential side bearing.
Replace the differential side bearing.

Damage of the drive pinion or ring gear.
Replace the drive pinion and ring gear together.

Excessive run-out of the ring gear.
Replace the drive pinion and the ring gear together.

Worn of the drive pinion and ring gear.
Replace the drive pinion and ring gear together.

Worn of the wheel bearing.
Replace the wheel bearing.

Damage or worn of the drive shaft joint and bellows (UJ and DOJ).
Replace the drive shaft joint and bellows.

Note

  • Vibration from the rear axle

Worn of the rear hub bearing.
Replace the rear hub bearing.

Bending of the rear axle shaft.
Replace the rear axle shaft.

Note

  • Abnormal sound from the rear axle
  • Abnormal sound during vehicle engine driving

Lack of gear oil.
Refill the gear oil.

Wrong gear oil or gear oil degradation.
Replace the gear oil.

Excessive or insufficient backlash between the drive pinion and ring gear.
Adjust the backlash.

Looseness of the drive pinion bearing.
Adjust the drive pinion bearing preload.

Worn of the drive pinion bearing.
Replace the drive pinion bearing.

Looseness of the differential side bearing.
Adjust the differential side bearing preload.

Worn of the differential side bearing.
Replace the differential side bearing.

Damage of the drive pinion or ring gear.
Replace the drive pinion and ring gear together.

Excessive run-out of the ring gear.
Replace the drive pinion and the ring gear together.

Worn of the drive pinion and ring gear.
Replace the drive pinion and ring gear together.

Note

  • Abnormal sound during vehicle coasting

Insufficient backlash of the drive pinion and ring gear.
Adjust the backlash.

Damage of the drive pinion gear or the ring gear.
Replace the ring gear and the drive pinion gear together.

Note

  • Intermittent noise

Excessive play of the axle shaft spline.
Replace the shaft.

Ring gear distortion.
Replace the drive pinion and ring gear together.

Looseness of the differential cage bolt.
Tighten the differential cage bolt.

Foreign matter in the axle case.
Remove the foreign matter.

Note

  • Abnormal sound during vehicle turning

Worn of the rear axle shaft spline.
Replace the rear axle shaft.

Worn of the side gear.
Replace the side gear.

Worn of the differential pinion.
Replace the differential pinion.

Worn of the differential cross pin.
Replace the differential cross pin.

Note

  • Constant abnormal sound

Bending of the rear axle shaft.
Replace the rear axle shaft.

Worn of the drive pinion gear or the ring gear tooth.
Replace the drive pinion and the ring gear together.

Worn of the drive pinion bearings or the differential side bearing.
Replace the drive pinion bearings or the differential side bearing.

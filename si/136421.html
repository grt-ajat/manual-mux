DTC B0019[0E] (Flash Code 16) Passenger Airbag Circuit Low Resistance (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B0019[0E] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "209.Description Restraints 22C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-SRS controls".

2. Inspection of harness connector

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Verify that the passenger airbag circuit harness connector is not damaged.

If the harness connector is damaged, replace the applicable harness connector. Go to Repair verification.

If the harness connector is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Disconnect the passenger airbag harness connector.

Connect an appropriate adapter from the connector test adapter kit to the harness connector.


SST: 5-8840-2958-0 - SRS adapter for load tool

Connect the adapter and the SRS alternative load.


SST: 5-8840-2856-0 - SRS alternative load

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, go to Passenger airbag replacement.

If a DTC is set, go to Inspection for short together between passenger airbag circuit lines.

4. Inspection for short together between passenger airbag circuit lines

Inspect for a short together between the passenger airbag Low circuit and High circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to SRS control unit replacement.

5. Passenger airbag replacement

Replace the passenger airbag. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) passenger air bag assembly removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) passenger air bag assembly installation".

6. SRS control unit replacement

Replace the SRS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit installation".

2. DTC B0019[0E] repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts and harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If the DTC is resolved, clear the DTC with a scan tool.

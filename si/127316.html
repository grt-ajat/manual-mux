2nd seat removal (All models)

1. 2nd seat removal

1. Remove the leg cover from the 2nd seat.

  1. Leg cover

2. Remove the 2nd seat from the floor.

Note

  • Fold back the carpet to access the retaining bolts.
  • Remove the seat retaining bolts.

  1. 2nd seat (LH)
  2. 2nd seat (RH)
  3. Carpet
  4. Bolt

2. Buckle side 2nd seat belt removal

1. Remove the buckle side 2nd seat belt from the seat.

(Except for center seat belt)

Refer to "8.Restraints 8C.Seat Belt Systems(All models) buckle side 2nd seat belt removal".

3. Tongue side 2nd seat belt removal

1. Remove the tongue side 2nd seat belt from the seat.

(For center seat belt)

Refer to "8.Restraints 8C.Seat Belt Systems(All models) tongue side 2nd seat belt (center) removal".

4. Buckle side 2nd seat belt removal

1. Remove the buckle side 2nd seat belt from the seat.

(For center seat belt)

Refer to "8.Restraints 8C.Seat Belt Systems(All models) buckle side 2nd seat belt (center) removal".

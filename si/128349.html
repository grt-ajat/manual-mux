Automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light always on (TB-50LS)

1. Automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light always on description of function

The automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light comes on when the ignition switch is turned ON, and then it turns off when the ATF temperature is 150 ˚C (302 ˚ F) or below. When the TCM detects the "ON" signal of the ignition switch, it lights the warning light for approximately 3 seconds for a valve check.

Function of automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light
The automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light is installed inside the instrument panel cluster and illuminates when the ATF temperature becomes high to notify the driver that the vehicle needs to be stopped to prevent the transmission from being damaged.
If the warning light illuminates responding to an instruction from the TCM, the ATF temperature has become high.

Lighting conditions of automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light
The automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light illuminates for approx. 3 seconds after the ignition switch is turned ON.
When the ATF temperature is 150˚C (302˚F) or higher, it remains on.

2. Automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light always on

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the transmission internal harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Verify lighting of the automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light.

If the automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light illuminates, go to Inspection for short circuit in transmission fluid temperature sensor 1 signal circuit.

If the automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light turns off, go to ATF cooling system inspection.

3. ATF cooling system inspection

Inspect the ATF cooling system for the following conditions, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Bending, clogging of the ATF cooler pipe
Damage to the radiator

If it is normal, go to Inspection of transmission internal harness.

4. Inspection of transmission internal harness

Inspect the transmission fluid temperature sensor 1 signal circuit in the transmission internal harness for a short to ground or the low reference circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, replace the transmission internal harness. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly reassembly".

5. Inspection for short circuit in transmission fluid temperature sensor 1 signal circuit

Inspect the transmission fluid temperature sensor 1 signal circuit for a short to ground or the low reference circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM Replacement.

6. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

7. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the harness connectors that have been disconnected.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Check the condition of the automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light.

Confirm that the automatic transmission fluid temperature warning light turns off after 3 seconds.

If it does not turn off, go to Circuit diagnosis.

Accelerator pedal assembly inspection (4JK1)

1. Accelerator pedal assembly inspection

1. Connect a scan tool to vehicle.

2. Turn ON the ignition switch.

3. Adjust the accelerator pedal position sensor.

Note

  • Check the voltage of the accelerator pedal position sensor as shown in the table below.

1Pedal at reset0： 0.75 to 0.85 V
1Pedal at full travel1： 3.12 to 3.22 V
2Pedal at reset0： 1.55 to 1.65 V
2Pedal at full travel1： 3.95 to 4.05 V

DTC B1110 Vehicle Speed Invalid Data (Passive Entry and Start System)

1. DTC B1110 description of DTC

The PESS control unit uses the vehicle speed signal received from the EHCU and controls power mode switches.
If the PESS control unit detects a malfunction in the vehicle speed signal from the EHCU, the DTC is set.

Note

  • Although this DTC is set, the engine can be started using emergency starting.

2. DTC B1110 condition for setting the DTC

The PESS control unit detects a malfunction in the vehicle speed signal.

3. DTC B1110 action taken when the DTC sets

When the power mode is ON:
The PESS warning light illuminates.

When the engine start/stop button is pressed:
The PESS warning light illuminates for 10 seconds.
"CHECK SYSTEM" will be displayed on the multi-information display for 10 seconds.
The buzzer built into the instrument panel cluster will sound 3 times.

When the door locks are commanded to lock or unlock:
The PESS warning light illuminates for 10 seconds.
"CHECK SYSTEM" will be displayed on the multi-information display for 10 seconds.
The external buzzer will sound for 3 seconds.

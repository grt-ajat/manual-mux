Barometric pressure sensor inspection (4JJ1)

1. Barometric pressure sensor inspection

1. Inspect the atmospheric pressure sensor.

Note

  • Confirm that the barometric pressure sensor is securely installed.
  • Inspect the sensor and connector sections for damage and dirt.

2. Connect the harness connector to the barometric pressure sensor.

3. Connect the battery ground cable to the battery.

4. Turn ON the ignition switch.

5. Check the Barometric Pressure (BARO) display of a scan tool.

Note

  • The barometric pressure in the scan tool normally indicates a value, which is close to the surrounding barometric pressure.

Note

  • If the difference between the value shown for the Barometric Pressure (BARO) display and the surrounding barometric pressure is great, replace the barometric pressure sensor.

6. Turn OFF the ignition switch.

7. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect it within 1 minute after the ignition switch is turned OFF.

8. Disconnect the harness connector from the barometric pressure sensor.

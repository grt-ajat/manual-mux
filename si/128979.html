Lost communication with the EHCU (All models)

1. Lost communication with the EHCU description of function

The ECM, TCM, DRM, TCCM, EHCU, ICU, SRS control unit, BCM, and instrument panel cluster communicate with the scan tool via the CAN communication bus. The EGR valve and auto A/C control unit use the same CAN communication bus when the vehicle is running, but do not use communication with the scan tool.

2. Lost communication with the EHCU

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. Inspection of EHCU harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the EHCU harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the EHCU harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in ignition power supply circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit and the ground circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in CAN circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in ignition power supply circuit

Inspect the ABS 10 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the ABS 10 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If the fuse is normal, repair any open circuit or high resistance in the ignition power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

6. Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, repair the connection status of the ground terminal. Go to Repair verification.

7. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in CAN circuit

Inspect the connection status of the CAN circuit junction connector between the EHCU and the DLC, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the CAN circuit between the EHCU and the DLC or for a short together between CAN circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to EHCU replacement.

8. EHCU replacement

Replace the EHCU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

9. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected fuses and harness connectors.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Check if communication with the EHCU can be established with a scan tool.

If communication cannot be established, go to Inspection of EHCU harness connector.

DTC B0019[01] (Flash Code 19) Passenger Airbag Circuit Short to Battery Voltage (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger))

1. DTC B0019[01] description of DTC

When the ignition switch is turned ON, the SRS control unit performs the test to diagnose serious functional failures inside the SRS control unit. If this test is passed successfully, the activation circuit voltage is measured to confirm that the voltage is within the normal voltage range.
Then, the SRS control unit performs the resistance measurement test. It sends a minute current of a specified amount in between both of the passenger airbag low terminal and high terminal. The SRS control unit calculates the composite resistance of the passenger airbag, the passenger airbag high and low circuits, and connector terminal contact points by measuring the voltage difference between the passenger airbag low terminal and high terminal.

2. DTC B0019[01] condition for setting the DTC

A voltage of 2.5 V or more is detected in the passenger airbag circuit for at least 4 seconds.

3. DTC B0019[01] action taken when the DTC sets

The SRS control unit illuminates the SRS airbag warning light.

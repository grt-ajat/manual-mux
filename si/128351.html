Lost communication with the TCM (TB-50LS)

1. Lost communication with the TCM description of function

The ECM, TCM, DRM, TCCM, EHCU, ICU, SRS control unit, BCM, and instrument panel cluster communicate with the scan tool via the CAN communication bus. The EGR valve and auto A/C control unit use the same CAN communication bus when the vehicle is running, but do not use communication with the scan tool.

2. Lost communication with the TCM

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Inspection of TCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the TCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the TCM harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in ignition power supply circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test light between the ignition power supply circuit and the ground circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to CAN serial data circuit inspection.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in ignition power supply circuit

Inspect the TCM 15 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the TCM 15 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If the fuse is normal, repair any open circuit or high resistance in the ignition power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

6. Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, repair the connection status of the ground terminal. Go to Repair verification.

7. CAN serial data circuit inspection

Inspect the connection status of the CAN circuit junction connector between the TCM and the DLC, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the CAN circuit between the TCM and the DLC or for a short circuit between CAN circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM Replacement.

8. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

9. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the fuses and harness connectors that have been disconnected.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Using the scan tool, communicate with the TCM.

If communication is not available, go to Inspection of TCM harness connector.

Inspection of steering free play (All models)

1. Inspection of steering free play

Inspection of free play in the steering wheel
Position the tires so that they are pointing straight forward, and turn the steering wheel in both directions until the tires start to move, and check the amount of play in the steering wheel.

Note

  • The free play in the wheel should be checked with the engine running.

： 10 to 30 mm { 0.39 to 1.18 in } Free play

Also, check for play and looseness by moving the steering wheel back, forth, left, and right. During a test drive, check for hard steering, steering shimmy and a tendency to be pulled toward one side.

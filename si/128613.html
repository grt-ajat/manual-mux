Lost communication with the BCM (BCM with PESS)

1. Lost communication with the BCM description of function

The ECM, TCM, DRM, TCCM, EHCU, ICU, SRS control unit, BCM, instrument panel cluster, and PESS control unit communicate with the scan tool via the CAN communication bus. The EGR valve and the auto A/C control unit use the same CAN communication bus when the vehicle is running, but do not use it for communication with the scan tool.

2. Lost communication with the BCM

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "223.Description Body Control 36C.Functional Inspection(BCM with PESS) diagnostic system check-body controls".

2. Inspection of BCM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the BCM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the BCM harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in ignition power supply circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the ignition power supply circuit and the ground circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in CAN circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in ignition power supply circuit

Inspect the ELEC IG1 fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the ELEC IG1 fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If the fuse is normal, repair any open circuit or high resistance in the ignition power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

6. Inspection for open circuit in ground circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, repair the connection status of the ground terminal. Go to Repair verification.

7. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in CAN circuit

Inspect the connection status of the CAN circuit junction connector between the BCM and the DLC, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the CAN circuit between the BCM and the DLC or for a short together between CAN circuits, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to BCM replacement.

8. BCM replacement

Replace the BCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM installation".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9E.Instrumentation, Driver Info.(All models) BCM setting".

9. Repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected fuses and harness connectors.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Verify that communication with the BCM can be established with a scan tool.

If communication cannot be established, go to Inspection of BCM harness connector.

DTC P0204 (Flash Code 274) Injector Circuit - Cylinder 4 (4JJ1)

1. DTC P0204 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "202.Description Engine 15C.Functional Inspection(4JJ1) diagnostic system check-engine controls".

2. Inspection of cylinder No. 4 injector harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the cylinder No. 4 injector harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of cylinder No. 4 injector.

3. Inspection of cylinder No. 4 injector

Measure the resistance of the cylinder No. 4 injector solenoid.

Standard： 2.0 Ω

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Cylinder No. 4 injector replacement.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis.

4. Circuit diagnosis

Disconnect the cylinder No. 1 and No. 4 injector harness connectors.

Measure the resistance of the charge voltage circuit between the cylinder No. 1 and No. 4 injector.

Standard： 1.0 Ω

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in charge voltage circuit.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in control circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit in charge voltage circuit

Inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the cylinder No. 4 injector charge voltage circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

6. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in control circuit

Inspect the cylinder No. 4 injector control circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of ECM harness connector.

7. Cylinder No. 4 injector replacement

Replace the cylinder No. 4 injector. Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • When the injector has been replace, set the injector ID code on the ECM.

Refer to "1.Engine 1C.Fuel System(4JJ1) injector removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1C.Fuel System(4JJ1) injector installation".

8. Inspection of ECM harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the ECM harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to ECM replacement.

9. ECM replacement

Replace the ECM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM removal".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM installation".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM setting".

Refer to "1.Engine 1J.Electrical(4JJ1) ECM writing".

2. DTC P0204 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Operate the vehicle within the conditions for setting the DTC or within the parameters observed in the Freeze Frame.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

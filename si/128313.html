DTC P0965 (Flash code 37) Pressure Control Solenoid B (SL2) Control Circuit Performance (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P0965 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Prioritized DTC

If DTC P0966 or P0967 is set as well, go to the applicable DTC diagnosis.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the transmission internal harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the pressure control solenoid B (SL2) high side control circuit and the body ground.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Note

  • If the circuit is normal, the test lamp illuminates, and then turns OFF.

If the test lamp remains ON or does not illuminate, go to Inspection for short circuit in high side control circuit.

If the test lamp illuminates and turns OFF, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

4. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the pressure control solenoid B (SL2) low side control circuit and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection 1 for short circuit in low side control circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

5. Circuit diagnosis 3

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the TCM harness connector.

Connect a test lamp between the pressure control solenoid B (SL2) low side control circuit and the battery power supply.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection 2 for short circuit in low side control circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of transmission internal harness.

6. Inspection for short circuit in high side control circuit

Inspect the high side control circuit for a short to the power supply circuit, ground, or the low side control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

7. Inspection 1 for short circuit in low side control circuit

Inspect for a short to the power supply circuit in the low side control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

8. Inspection 2 for short circuit in low side control circuit

Inspect for a short to ground in the low side control circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to TCM replacement.

9. Inspection of transmission internal harness

Inspect for a short circuit in the pressure control solenoid B (SL2) circuit of the transmission internal harness, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, replace the valve body. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly reassembly".

Caution

  • After replacing the valve assembly, clear the self-learning data with a scan tool.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly installation".

10. TCM replacement

Replace the TCM. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM installation".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) TCM setting".

2. DTC P0965 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Note

  • The ECM sets DTC P0700 when the TCM sets emission related DTC(s). Clean the ECM DTC after TCM DTC(s) is cleared.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Start the engine and drive the vehicle in the 5th gear of the D range.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

Scan tool does not power up (4JJ1)

1. Scan tool does not power up description of function

The DLC is a standardized 16-pole connector. Connector design and location are dictated by an industry-wide standard, and is required to provide the following.
The battery voltage of the scan tool power supply is terminal 16.
The scan tool power supply ground is terminal 4.
The command signal ground is terminal 5.
The scan tool will power up with the ignition OFF. However, some modules will not communicate unless the ignition is ON.

2. Scan tool does not power up

Note

  • Verify that the scan tool operates properly on another vehicle.

1. Circuit diagnosis

Check the LED power lamp of the IDSS interface.

If the lamp illuminates, go to Connection verification 1.

If the lamp does not illuminate, go to Connection verification 2.

2. Connection verification 1

Inspect the USB cable connection status of the computer and the IDSS interface, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Repair verification.

3. Connection verification 2

Inspect the smart cable connection status of the DLC and the IDSS interface, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Inspect the USB cable connection status of the computer and the IDSS interface, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of fuse.

4. Inspection of fuse

Inspect the METER (+B) 10 A fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the METER (+B) 10 A fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If the fuse is normal, go to Inspection of DLC.

5. Inspection of DLC

Inspect the spread of the DLC terminals or for missing terminals, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the DLC is normal, go to Inspection of power supply circuit.

6. Inspection of power supply circuit

Connect a test lamp between the DLC battery power supply circuit and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection of ground circuit.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, repair an open circuit in the battery power supply circuit. Go to Repair verification.

7. Inspection of ground circuit

Repair an open circuit, high resistance, or poor connections of the ground terminal in the DLC ground circuit. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, refer to Intermittent conditions of engine, and repair as necessary.

8. Repair verification

Connect a scan tool to the DLC.

Turn ON the scan tool.

If the scan tool does not power up, go to Circuit diagnosis.

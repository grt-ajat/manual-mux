DTC C0110[04] (Flash Code 68, 69) Motor relay malfunction or Open/short circuit detection (ABS/ESC)

1. DTC C0110[04] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the EHCU harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the EHCU motor power supply circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 10 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the reading is less than the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit in motor power supply circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Measure the voltage between the EHCU motor power supply circuit and the motor ground circuit.

Standard： 10 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection of EHCU harness connector.

If the reading is less than the specified value, inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the motor ground circuit between the EHCU and the body ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

4. Inspection for open circuit in motor power supply circuit

Inspect the ABS1/ESC1 50 A slow blow fuse, and repair as necessary. If the fuse blows out again, repair the short to ground in the circuit relating to the ABS1/ESC1 50 A slow blow fuse. Go to Repair verification.

If the fuse is normal, inspect for an open circuit or high resistance in the motor power supply circuit between the ABS1/ESC1 50 A slow blow fuse and the EHCU, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

5. Inspection of EHCU harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the EHCU harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to EHCU replacement.

6. EHCU replacement

Replace the EHCU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

2. DTC C0110[04] repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Start the engine and drive the vehicle.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

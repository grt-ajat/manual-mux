DTC B0084[39] (Flash Code 38) Front Airbag Sensor (Right) Disconnected (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B0084[39] description of DTC

The front airbag sensor detects a frontal collision. The SRS control unit supplies power source to the front airbag sensor. When an impact larger than a certain level is received to the front airbag sensor by a frontal collision, the impact is converted into electrical signals and transmitted to the SRS control unit. The SRS control unit detects a frontal collision by a signal from the front airbag sensor.
If any failure occurs in the right side front airbag sensor, the right side front airbag sensor power supply circuit or ground circuit, the SRS control unit sets this DTC.

2. DTC B0084[39] condition for setting the DTC

A voltage of 6 V or more is detected in the right front airbag sensor circuit for at least 1 second.
A resistance of 48 Ω or more is detected in the right front airbag sensor power supply circuit for at least 1 second.
A resistance of 810 Ω or less is detected in the right front airbag sensor circuit for at least 1 second.
A failure occurs in the right front airbag sensor.

3. DTC B0084[39] action taken when the DTC sets

The SRS control unit illuminates the SRS airbag warning light.

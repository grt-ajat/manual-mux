Abnormal noises from suspension (All models)

1. Abnormal noises from suspension diagnostics

Worn on the upper or lower link bushing.
Replace.

Loosening of the shock absorber fixing bolt.
Tighten it to the specified torque.

Loosening of the stabilizer fixing bolt and nut.
Tighten them to the specified torque.

Defective shock absorber.
Replace.

Insufficient lubrication of the upper or lower ball joint.
Replace.

Damage to the upper or lower ball joint.
Replace.

Worn on the steering linkage and steering gear.
Replace.

Improper adjustment or worn of the wheel bearing.
Adjust or replace the hub bearing.

Loosening of the steering gear fixing bolt and linkage.
Tighten them to the specified torque.

Improper lubrication and greasing of the steering linkage.
Replace.

Poor wheel alignment.
Adjust the wheel alignment.

Deformation of the knuckle spring and steering linkage.
Replace.

Looseness of the suspension system fastening bolts and nuts
Tighten to the specified torque.

Improper tire air pressure.
Adjust the tire air pressure.

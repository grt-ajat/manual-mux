DTC B001A[02] (Flash Code 92) Lap Pretensioner Left Circuit Short to GND (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B001A[02] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "209.Description Restraints 22C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-SRS controls".

2. Harness connector inspection

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Check whether the lap side pretensioner (LH) circuit harness connector is broken.

If the harness connector is broken, replace the applicable harness connector. Go to Repair verification.

If the harness connector is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Disconnect the lap side pretensioner (LH) harness connector.

Connect an appropriate adapter from the connector test adapter kit to the harness connector.


SST: 5-8840-2857-0 - adapter harness kit

Connect the adapter and the SRS alternative load.


SST: 5-8840-2856-0 - SRS alternative load

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, go to Lap side pretensioner (LH) replacement.

If a DTC is set, go to Inspection for short to ground in lap side pretensioner (LH) circuit.

4. Inspection for short to ground in lap side pretensioner (LH) circuit

Inspect the lap side pretensioner (LH) Low circuit and lap side pretensioner (LH) High circuit for a short to ground, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to SRS control unit replacement.

5. Lap side pretensioner (LH) replacement

Replace the lap side pretensioner (LH). Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) seat belt with pretensioner removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) seat belt with pretensioner installation".

6. SRS control unit replacement

Replace the SRS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit installation".

2. DTC B001A[02] repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts and harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If the DTC is resolved, clear the DTC with a scan tool.

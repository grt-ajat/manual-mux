DTC P0777 (Flash code 57) Pressure Control Solenoid B (SL2) Stuck On (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P0777 diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) diagnostic system check-transmission controls".

2. Inspection of ATF

Inspect the amount and contamination level of the ATF and adjust or replace as necessary.

3. Prioritized DTC

If a DTC other than DTC P0747, P0751, P0752, P0756, P0757, P0771, P0772, P0777, or P0781 is set as well, go to the applicable DTC diagnosis.

4. Inspection of sensor harness

Inspect all circuits that connect to the turbine speed sensor and the output speed sensor for the following conditions, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Too close to the wiring or parts of the fuel injection system.
Too close to any electronic device that was additionally installed.

If it is normal, go to Inspection of harness connector.

5. Inspection of harness connector

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the turbine speed sensor, the output speed sensor, and the TCM harness connector.

Inspect the connection status of each harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to Inspection of sensor.

6. Inspection of sensor

Inspect the turbine speed sensor and the output speed sensor for the following conditions, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Sensor damage
Looseness or improper installation of sensor
Excessive gap
Foreign material passing between turbine speed sensor and forward clutch assembly or direct clutch assembly
Foreign material passing between output speed sensor and parking lock gear
Damage to forward clutch assembly and parking lock gear

If it is normal, go to Line pressure test.

7. Line pressure test

Perform the line pressure test, and repair if the reading is outside the specified range. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "206.Description Transmission, Transaxle 19C.Functional Inspection(TB-50LS) measurement of the automatic transmission operating pressure".

If it is normal, go to Inspection of transmission.

8. Transmission inspection

Remove the transmission oil pan.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly disassembly".

Inspect whether there is any foreign material in the oil pan and the transmission, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If it is normal, inspect the valve body, each clutch, and each brake, and repair or replace as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly removal".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly inspection".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) valve body assembly disassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) valve body assembly inspection".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) valve body assembly reassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly reassembly".

Refer to "5.Transmission, Transaxle 5B.Automatic(TB-50LS) transmission assembly installation".

Caution

  • After replacing the valve body assembly, each clutch, or each brake, clear the self-learning data with a scan tool.

2. DTC P0777 repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Note

  • The ECM sets DTC P0700 when the TCM sets emission related DTC(s). Clean the ECM DTC after TCM DTC(s) is cleared.

Turn OFF the ignition switch for at least 30 seconds.

Start the engine and drive the vehicle in the 5th gear of the D range.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

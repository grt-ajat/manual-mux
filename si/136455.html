DTC B0021[02] (Flash Code 82) Side Airbag (Right) Circuit Short to GND (Air Bag (Driver/Passenger/Side/Roof))

1. DTC B0021[02] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "209.Description Restraints 22C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check-SRS controls".

2. Inspection of harness connector

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Inspect the side airbag (Right) circuit harness connector for damage.

If the harness connector is damaged, replace the applicable harness connector. Go to Repair verification.

If the harness connector is normal, go to Circuit diagnosis 1.

3. Circuit diagnosis 1

Disconnect the side airbag (Right) harness connector.

Connect the SRS adapter for load tool to the harness connector.


SST: 5-8840-2982-0 - SRS adapter for load tool

Connect the adapter and the SRS alternative load.


SST: 5-8840-2856-0 - SRS alternative load

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If a DTC is not set, go to Side airbag (Right) replacement.

If a DTC is set, go to Inspection for short to ground in side airbag (Right) circuit.

4. Inspection for short to ground in side airbag (Right) circuit

Inspect for a short to ground in the side airbag (Right) Low circuit and a short to ground in the side airbag (Right) High circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to SRS control unit replacement.

5. Side airbag (Right) replacement

Replace the side airbag (Right). Go to Repair verification.

Note

  • The side airbag cannot be replaced separately.
  • Replace the seat assembly.

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9I.Seats(All models) front seat removal".

Refer to "9.Body, Cab, Accessories 9I.Seats(All models) front seat installation".

6. SRS control unit replacement

Replace the SRS control unit. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit removal".

Refer to "8.Restraints 8B.Air Bag Systems(All models) SRS control unit installation".

2. DTC B0021[02] repair verification

Reconnect all of the disconnected parts and harness connectors.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

If the DTC is resolved, clear the DTC with a scan tool.

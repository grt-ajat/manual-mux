DTC P2758 (Flash code 53) TCC Pressure Control Solenoid (SLU) Control Circuit Stuck On (TB-50LS)

1. DTC P2758 description of DTC

The torque converter clutch pressure control solenoid (SLU) is installed on the valve body assembly and adjusts the control oil pressure that operates the lock-up clutch. The TCM controls the torque converter clutch pressure control solenoid (SLU) according to the accelerator pedal position, the output shaft rotation, etc. The TCM sets a DTC when it detects the lock-up ON state while the lock-up clutch OFF instruction has been given.

2. DTC P2758 condition for setting the DTC

DTCs P0707, P0708, P0717, P0722, P0882, P0883, P0961, P0962, P0963, P0965, P0966, P0967, P0973, P0974, P0976, P0977, P0985, P0986, P1790, P1791, P2719, P2720, P2721, P2762, P2763, P2764, U0073 and U0100 are not set.
The battery voltage is 10.5 V or more.
The engine speed is 550 rpm or more.
The shift position is D.
The engine coolant temperature is 40°C (104°F) or more.
The 4L switch is OFF.
All of the following conditions are satisfied.
The vehicle speed is 10 km/h (6 mph) or more.
The desired gear position is the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, or 5th gear.
The desired lock-up oil pressure is 4 kPa (0.04 kgf/cm2 / 1 psi) or less.
The current accelerator position is larger than the accelerator position calculated by the TCM.
The current engine torque is larger than the engine torque calculated by the TCM.
The TCM detects that the engine speed is lower than the turbine speed by 35.2 rpm or more for 2 seconds.

3. DTC P2758 action taken when the DTC sets

Note

  • Euro4 model

When the diagnostic runs and fails, the TCM flashes the check trans warning light on the second consecutive driving cycle.
When the diagnostic runs and fails, the TCM requests to illuminate the MIL on the second consecutive driving cycle.
When the diagnostic reports a failure on the second consecutive driving cycle, the TCM stores this information in the Failure Records.
The ECM records the operating conditions at the time the diagnostic fails. The ECM stores this information in the Freeze Frame/Failure Records.
The ECM stores DTC P0700 in ECM history during the second consecutive driving cycle in which the diagnostic fails.
The TCM inhibits the squat control.
The TCM inhibits the self-learning control.
The TCM inhibits the 2nd start control.
When the vehicle speed becomes 10 km/h (6 mph) or lower, the gear position is fixed in the 1st gear.

Note

  • Euro2 and Euro3 model

The TCM flashes the check trans warning light on the second consecutive driving cycle that the diagnostic runs and fails.
If the diagnostic reports a failure on the second consecutive driving cycle, the TCM stores this information in the Failure Records.
The TCM inhibits the squat control.
The TCM inhibits the self-learning control.
The TCM inhibits the 2nd start control.
When the vehicle speed becomes 10 km/h (6 mph) or lower, the gear position is fixed in the 1st gear.

Transmission oil inspection (MUX)

1. Transmission oil inspection

Note

  • Inspection of the transmission oil

Remove the filler plug.

Insert your finger into the filler plug hole to check if the oil is filled up to the lower surface of the hole.

  1. Filler plug
  2. Drain plug
  3. Filler plug hole
  4. Lower surface of the hole
  5. Oil surface

Replenish the oil if the oil level is below the specified level.

Oil for use = Besco transaxle oil (5W-30)

Tighten the filler plug to the specified torque.

Replace with a new O-ring.

Tightening torque： 39 N・m { 4.0 kgf・m / 29 lb・ft }

Note

  • Replacement of the transmission oil

Start the engine and warm up the transmission body sufficiently.

Stop the engine, remove the drain plug and the filler plug, and then drain the oil.

When the oil temperature is high, be careful so as not to burn yourself.

Tighten the drain plug to the specified torque.

Replace with a new O-ring.

Tightening torque： 39 N・m { 4.0 kgf・m / 29 lb・ft }

From the filler plug hole, fill oil up to the lower surface of the filler plug hole.

Oil for use = Besco transaxle oil (5W-30)

Oil capacity [Reference value]
： 3.0 L { 0.79 US gal / 0.66 Imp.gal }

Tighten the filler plug to the specified torque.

Replace with a new O-ring.

Tightening torque： 39 N・m { 4.0 kgf・m / 29 lb・ft }

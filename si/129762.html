Center in-vehicle antenna removal (All models)

1. Battery ground cable disconnect

1. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

2. Console box removal

Note

  • The following applies to center console type manual transmissions.

1. Remove the shift lever knob from the shift lever.

Note

  • The following applies to the center console type.

2. Remove the shift console cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to manual transmissions.

  1. Shift lever knob
  2. Shift console cover
  3. Cover (2WD models)
  4. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • The following applies to automatic transmissions.

  1. Shift console cover
  2. Cover (2WD models)
  3. 4WD switch (4WD models)

Note

  • Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector.
  • The following applies to 2WD center console types.

3. Remove the cover from the console box.

Note

  • The following applies to 4WD center console types.

4. Remove the 4WD switch from the console box.

Note

  • Disconnect the 4WD switch connector.
  • The following applies to the center console type.

5. Remove the console box from the floor.

Note

  • Remove the parking brake cover from the console box.
  • Remove the 2 clips and the 1 screw.
  • Open the console box cover and remove the 2 screws.
  • The following applies to models equipped with PESS.
  • Disconnect the connector for the in-vehicle antenna.
  • Remove the console box backward.

  1. Cover (2WD models)
  2. 4WD switch (4WD models)
  3. Screw
  4. Parking brake cover
  5. Console box
  6. Clip

3. In-vehicle antenna removal

1. Remove the in-vehicle antenna from the console box.

Note

  • Remove the 2 screws.
  • Remove the 4 clips used for fastening the harness.

  1. In-vehicle antenna
  2. Harness connector
  3. Console box

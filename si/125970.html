Fuel tube removal (4JJ1)

1. Fuel tube safety information

Caution

  • Do not light a match, or do not generate a flame.
  • Avoid fire at the workshop, and prevent inflammables from catching fire.
  • Disconnect the battery ground cable and prevent electric short circuit.
  • When performing welding or similar heat-generating work, preprocess the pipelines or associated parts in order to protect them from thermal damage or spattering.
  • Do not contact the assembly with the electrolyte of the battery.
  • Do not wipe the assembly with the cloth that has been used to wipe the spilled electrolyte.
  • Do not use the piping to which the electrolyte of the battery was scattered or the cloth that was soaked with the electrolyte of the battery after wiping the piping.

2. Battery ground cable disconnect

1. Open the engine hood assembly.

2. Disconnect the battery ground cable from the battery.

Caution

  • Do not disconnect within 1 minute after turning OFF the ignition switch.

3. Raise vehicle using the jack.

3. Fuel filler cap removal

1. Remove the fuel filler cap from the filler neck.

Note

  • Gradually loosen the fuel filler cap.

Caution

  • Be careful with fuel spouts due to a pressure change in the fuel tank.
  • Cover the opening of the filler neck to prevent foreign material from getting inside.

4. Fuel tube disconnect

1. Disconnect the fuel feed tube from the fuel hose.

2. Disconnect the fuel return tube from the fuel hose.

  1. Fuel feed tube
  2. Fuel return tube

5. Fuel tank removal

1. Disconnect the breather hose from the filler neck.

2. Disconnect the fuel filler hose from the filler neck.

3. Disconnect the evaporator hose from the filler neck.

Note

  • Remove the clip and disconnect the breather hose, filler hose, and evaporator hose.

  1. Fuel filler hose
  2. Evaporator hose
  3. Breather hose

4. Hold the fuel tank using the lifter.

Note

  • Support the bottom of the fuel tank using the lifter.

5. Remove the fuel tank band from the frame.

6. Disconnect the connector from the fuel tank unit.

7. Remove the fuel tank from vehicle.

Note

  • Lower the lifter and remove the fuel tank from the vehicle.

Caution

  • Do not damage the hose and the tube through contact with other parts.

6. Fuel tube removal

1. Clean the fuel tube using air.

Note

  • Remove the dirt on the quick connector fitting by using compressed air.

2. Remove the fuel tube from the fuel tank unit.

Note

  • To remove the quick connector, hold the quick connector with one hand while pushing the release button of the connector and pull out the connector with the other hand.

  1. Release button

Caution

  • Do not use any tool.
  • When disconnecting the connector, remove it with bare hands.
  • Use lubricating oil, or push and pull the connector until the pipe is disconnected.

Caution

  • Cover the removed connector with a plastic bag to prevent dust or rain water from entering.

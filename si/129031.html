DTC C0800[03] (Flash Code 66, 67, 77) Voltage drop detection (All models)

1. DTC C0800[03] description of DTC

The EHCU monitors the system voltage. If the reading for the EHCU power supply voltage is abnormally low, the DTC is set.

2. DTC C0800[03] condition for setting the DTC

When any of the following conditions are met.
The EHCU detects a malfunction of the solenoid relay monitor.
The EHCU detects a malfunction of the motor relay monitor.
The EHCU detects a decrease of ignition power source voltage
The EHCU detects a decrease of power source voltage on wheel speed sensor.

3. DTC C0800[03] action taken when the DTC sets

The ABS warning light illuminates.
The brake system warning light illuminates.
The ESC warning light illuminates.
The EHCU inhibits the ABS control.
The EHCU inhibits the EBD control.
The EHCU inhibits the TCS control.
The EHCU inhibits the ESC control.

DTC C0035[06] (Flash Code 21) FL Wheel Speed Sensor Circuit malfunction (ABS/ESC)

1. DTC C0035[06] diagnostics

1. Diagnostic system check

Refer to "205.Description Brakes 18C.Functional Inspection(All models) diagnostic system check- ABS/EBD/TCS/ESC controls".

2. Circuit diagnosis 1

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Disconnect the front left wheel speed sensor harness connector.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Measure the voltage between the front left wheel speed sensor power supply circuit and the body ground.

Standard： 8 V

If the reading is more than or equal to the specified value, go to Circuit diagnosis 2.

If the reading is less than or equal to the specified value, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in power supply circuit.

3. Circuit diagnosis 2

Connect a test lamp between the front left wheel speed sensor signal circuit and the body ground.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Circuit diagnosis 3.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in signal circuit.

4. Circuit diagnosis 3

Leave the test lamp connected.

Disconnect the EHCU harness connector.

If the test lamp does not illuminate, go to Inspection of front left wheel speed sensor harness connector.

If the test lamp illuminates, go to Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit.

5. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in power supply circuit

Inspect the front left wheel speed sensor power supply circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, a short to ground or a short to the signal circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of EHCU harness connector.

6. Inspection for open circuit and short circuit in signal circuit

Inspect the front left wheel speed sensor signal circuit for an open circuit, high resistance, or a short to the ground circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of EHCU harness connector.

7. Inspection for short circuit in signal circuit

Inspect the front left wheel speed sensor signal circuit for a short to the power supply circuit, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the circuit is normal, go to Inspection of EHCU harness connector.

8. Inspection of front left wheel speed sensor harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the front left wheel speed sensor harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If it is normal, replace the front left wheel speed sensor. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) front wheel speed sensor removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) front wheel speed sensor installation".

9. Inspection of EHCU harness connector

Inspect the connection status of the EHCU harness connector, and repair as necessary. Go to Repair verification.

If the connection status is normal, go to EHCU replacement.

10. EHCU replacement

Replace the EHCU. Go to Repair verification.

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU removal".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU installation".

Refer to "4.Brakes 4D.ABS(All models) EHCU setting".

2. DTC C0035[06] repair verification

Clear the DTC with a scan tool.

Turn OFF the ignition switch.

Turn ON the ignition switch.

Observe the DTC information with a scan tool.

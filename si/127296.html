Tail gate disassembly (All models)

1. Tail gate trim cover removal

1. Remove the tail gate trim cover from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the screw for the assist grip.
  • Pull out the 13 clips for the tail gate trim cover.

  1. Clip position
  2. Screw

2. Rear wiper arm removal

1. Remove the wiper arm from vehicle.

Note

  • Remove the nut and then remove the wiper arm and blade.

  1. Blade
  2. Wiper arm cover

3. Rear wiper motor removal

1. Remove the rear wiper motor from the tail gate.

Note

  • Disconnect the rear wiper motor connector.
  • Remove the 3 mounting bolts.

  1. Rear wiper motor
  2. Bolt

4. Tail gate garnish removal

1. Remove the tail gate garnish from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the 2 nuts and pull out the 4 clips.

  1. Nut
  2. Stud bolt
  3. Clip

5. Outside handle removal

1. Remove the outside handle from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the 2 fastening bolts.

2. Disconnect the harness connector from the tail gate harness.

  1. Outside handle
  2. Bolt

6. Tail gate lock removal

1. Remove the tail gate lock from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the fastening bolt and disconnect the connector.

  1. Tail gate lock
  2. Bolt

7. Tail stoplight removal

1. Open the tail gate.

2. Remove the cover from the tail gate.

  1. Cover
  2. Tail gate

3. Disconnect the connector from the socket.

4. Remove the tail stoplight from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the nut and clip from the tail gate.

  1. Nut
  2. Cap nut
  3. Taillight
  4. Clip

8. Tail gate hinge removal

1. Remove the tail gate hinge from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the mounting bolts.

  1. Tail gate hinge
  2. Bolt

9. Dove tail removal

1. Remove the dove tail from the tail gate.

Note

  • Remove the mounting bolts.

  1. Bolt
  2. Dovetail

10. Rear camera removal

Note

  • The following applies to models with a rear camera.

1. Remove the rear camera from the tail gate.

Note

  • Disconnect the rear camera harness connector.
  • Remove the screw to remove the rear camera.

  1. Grommet
  2. Screw
  3. Rear camera
